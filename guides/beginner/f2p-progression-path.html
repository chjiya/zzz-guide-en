📅 Day 1-7: The First Week

Goal: Reach Proxy Level 30, unlock all basic systems, and build your first team of level 30-40 characters.

Day 1: Start Strong

  1. Complete the tutorial: The tutorial gives you your first three characters: Anby (A-rank Electric Stun), Billy (A-rank Physical Attack), and Nicole (A-rank Ether Support). These three will carry you through the first 2 chapters.
  2. Pull the Beginner Banner: Use your free Master Tapes (blue) on the Beginner Banner. It costs half price for the first 50 pulls and guarantees one random S-rank from the standard pool at the end. Do NOT spend Polychromes on this.
  3. Push the main story: Complete Chapters 1 and 2 as fast as possible. This unlocks your daily commissions, Battle Pass, and most event content.
  4. Set up your video store: The Random Play video store is your passive income source. Set the movie schedule daily for free Dennies and investigation materials.

Day 2-3: Establish Your Routine

  1. Complete daily commissions: 60 Polychromes per day. Takes 5 minutes. Never skip this.
  2. Spend stamina on character materials: Focus on level-up materials for Anby, Billy, and Nicole. Don't farm Drive Discs yet — you'll get better ones at higher levels.
  3. Unlock Hollow Zero: Complete the Hollow Zero tutorial quest. This gives your first Bangboo and access to weekly Polychrome rewards.
  4. Join a community: Join the official ZZZ Discord and a few content creator communities. Good sources for team-building advice and banner pull recommendations.

Day 4-7: First Team Completion

  1. Replace Billy with Piper: Piper (A-rank Physical Anomaly, obtainable from events or standard banner) is a massive upgrade over Billy. She will be your main DPS for the foreseeable future. Level her to 40 as soon as possible.
  2. Build your first team: Piper (DPS) + Anby (Stun) + Nicole (Support). This team can clear all content up to Proxy Level 40.
  3. Complete all events: New player events give significant Polychromes and materials. Prioritize time-limited events first, then permanent content.
  4. Save Polychromes: By the end of Week 1, you should have approximately 4,000-6,000 Polychromes saved (25-37 pulls). Do not spend on standard or W-Engine banners.

📅 Week 2-4: Building Your First Real Team

Goal: Proxy Level 40-45, first team at level 50, unlock Shiyu Defense.

Target a Limited Banner Character

By Week 2-3, you should have enough Polychromes saved to pull for a limited S-rank. Prioritize Caesar above all others. If Caesar isn't available, save your pulls for her rerun — she is that important for F2P accounts. If you must pull something, Burnice is the next best option.

If you get Caesar: Replace Nicole in your main team with Caesar. Your team becomes Piper + Anby + Caesar. Caesar's shield (4,000+ HP at level 50) triples your team's survivability and her 1,000 ATK buff significantly increases Piper's damage.

If you don't get Caesar yet: Continue with Piper + Anby + Nicole. This team is strong enough to clear all pre-endgame content. At Proxy Level 40, you unlock the ability to farm Drive Discs efficiently — this is your next priority.

Drive Disc Farming (Proxy Level 35+)

At Proxy Level 35, the music store (Drive Disc domain) unlocks its highest tier. Start farming Drive Discs here:

  • Piper: 4-piece Fanged Metal (Physical DMG bonus) + 2-piece Freedom Blues (Anomaly Proficiency). Slot IV: Anomaly Proficiency. Slot V: Physical DMG%. Slot VI: ATK%.
  • Anby: 4-piece Shockstar Disco (Impact / Daze) + 2-piece any (Impact or Energy Regen). Slot IV-VI: Impact% (all three).
  • Caesar (if pulled): 4-piece Protective Harmony + 2-piece Swing Jazz. Slot IV: HP%. Slot V: HP%. Slot VI: Energy Regen.

📅 Month 2: Expanding Your Roster

Goal: Proxy Level 45-50, build a second team, start clearing Shiyu Defense Stable Node.

Month 2 is about building depth. You need two teams for Shiyu Defense, which will become your primary Polychrome income source going forward.

Team 1 (Your main): Piper + Anby + Caesar (or your best support). This team handles the harder floors.

Team 2 (Your secondary): Lucy + Nicole + Billy (or any remaining A-rank). This team handles the easier floors. Lucy provides a 600 ATK buff (at M6), Nicole provides DEF shred, and Billy does passable damage. This team won't clear Critical Node but is fine for Stable Node floors 1-5.

Better Team 2 options: If you pulled Burnice, build Burnice + Piper + Caesar and move Anby to team 2 (Anby + Lucy + Nicole). If you pulled Yanagi, build Yanagi + Piper + Seth (if you have him) or Yanagi + Grace + Rina. The key is to have two teams of level 50+ characters with decent Drive Discs.

Important Activities:

  • Clear Hollow Zero on all 3 difficulties (one-time Polychrome rewards).
  • Clear Shiyu Defense Stable Node floors 1-7 (one-time ~300 Polychromes).
  • Attempt Shiyu Defense Critical Node floors 5-6. Even B-rank clears give Polychromes.
  • Complete all Battle Pass missions. The free pass gives Master Tapes and upgrade materials.

📅 Month 3+: Endgame Preparation

Goal: Proxy Level 50+, two teams at level 55-60, consistent S-rank Shiyu Defense clears.

By Month 3, your account should look like this:

  • Team 1: Fully built level 60 team with +12-15 Drive Discs. Clears Shiyu Defense Critical Node floor 7 in 120-150 seconds.
  • Team 2: Level 55 team with +9-12 Drive Discs. Clears Critical Node floor 5-6 in 120-180 seconds.
  • Polychrome income: ~5,000-6,000 per month from all sources.
  • Limited characters: 2-3 S-ranks (including your guaranteed from Beginner Banner and any you pulled from limited banners).

From this point: You are established. Your priorities shift to optimizing your existing teams and saving for specific future characters. The general pull plan for Month 3+ is:

  1. Save for a specific upcoming limited S-rank that fits your team archetype.
  2. Consider pulling signature W-Engines for your main DPS characters.
  3. Invest in mindscapes for your most-used characters (Caesar M1, Burnice M1, Piper M6 are all excellent).

❓ FAQ

Q1: Can I reach endgame as a completely F2P player?

Absolutely. Zenless Zone Zero is very F2P-friendly compared to many gacha games. The game gives you an excellent A-rank core team (Piper, Lucy, Anby, Nicole) that can clear all content, including Shiyu Defense S-rank clears on most rotations. The only content you'll truly struggle with as F2P is the absolute highest tiers of Hollow Zero (Infernal Reap) and speedrunning records — but these reward mostly cosmetic items and bragging rights. All meaningful Polychrome income is accessible to F2P players.

Q2: Is it worth spending Polychromes on stamina refreshes?

Only the first stamina refresh per day (50 Polychromes) is worth considering. The second and third refreshes (75 and 100 Polychromes) are borderline. Any more than 3 refreshes is wasteful for F2P players. As a rule of thumb: if you're still building your first team (Month 1-2), do 0 refreshes. If you're in endgame (Month 3+), do 1 refresh daily if you have the spare Polychromes after accounting for your next desired character pull. One refresh per day costs 1,500 Polychromes per month (~9 pulls) — make sure you're not sacrificing a character guarantee for it.

Q3: How do I get Piper if I didn't get her from the beginner banner?

Piper is an A-rank agent available on all banners (limited, standard, and beginner). You can also get her from the Signal Shop rotation (costs 240 residual signals, available once every 2-3 months). If you don't have her yet, use Corin (A-rank Physical Attack) as your temporary DPS. Corin's stun-window damage is actually higher than Piper's, but her overall consistency is lower. You'll eventually get Piper from any banner pull — all A-ranks have a ~13% chance per 10-pull.

Q4: What's the most important thing to do every day?

Without question, it's the daily commissions. 60 Polychromes per day = 1,800 per month = approximately 11 pulls. Missing a single day costs you 0.375 pulls. The second most important thing is spending your stamina so it doesn't cap (it caps at 240 and refills at 1 per 6 minutes). Wasted stamina is wasted resources — even if you don't need anything specific, run the highest material domain and stockpile. Never let your stamina sit at full for more than an hour.
