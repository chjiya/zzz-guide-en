📋 Why Polychrome Management Matters

In Zenless Zone Zero, a limited S-rank agent costs approximately 14,400 Polychrome (90 pulls) on average to guarantee (hard pity 180, soft pity ~75). With F2P players earning roughly 4,200-4,800 Polychrome per patch, you can only guarantee 1 limited S-rank agent every 3-4 patches. Smart Polychrome management is the difference between having a stacked account and constantly missing the characters you want.

📊 Pull Economy Basics

  • 1 Pull = 160 Polychrome
  • Hard Pity: 90 pulls for S-rank, 10 pulls for A-rank
  • Soft Pity: S-rank rate increases dramatically after 75 pulls
  • 50/50: First S-rank on a limited banner has 50% chance to be the featured agent. If you lose, your next S-rank is guaranteed to be the featured agent.
  • Pity Carries Over: Banner pity carries forward to the next limited banner of the same type.

🎯 Pull Planning Strategy

Agent Priority Framework

When deciding whether to pull for a character, evaluate on four axes:

  1. Team Archetype Value: Does this agent enable a new team archetype (e.g., Miyabi enables Frost teams, Caesar enables shield teams)? Enablers are more valuable than pure upgrades.
  2. Account Coverage: Do you already have a strong team for this element/role? A new DPS for an element you already cover is less valuable than a support or stunner that improves your existing teams.
  3. F2P Friendliness: Does the agent work at M0W0 (no duplicates, no signature W-Engine)? Agents like Caesar and Burnice are excellent even without any investment beyond their base copy.
  4. Longevity: Is this agent likely to stay relevant? Universal supports (Nicole, Lucy, Ruan Mei type) remain meta much longer than pure DPS characters.

W-Engine Pull Priority

Signature W-Engines are powerful but expensive (75/75 hard pity). As a F2P or low spender, your pull plan should be:

  • Never pull on W-Engine banners unless you have 180+ pulls saved after securing the agent.
  • F2P alternatives are often 85-90% as effective as signature W-Engines. The gap is rarely worth 75 pulls.
  • Exception: Miyabi's Frost Moon's Grace. F2P Ice W-Engines are weak for her. If you can only pull one W-Engine, make it hers.
  • Starlight Engine (craftable) is excellent for Attacker agents and only 5-8% behind most signature options.

⚠️ Common Spending Mistakes

  • Pulling on Standard Banner with Polychrome: Never do this. Use only free Master Tapes.
  • Chasing A-rank Mindscapes: A-rank dupes come naturally. Don't pull a banner you don't want the S-rank for, just to get A-rank copies.
  • Skipping the Monthly Pass: If you're spending at all, the Monthly Pass is 12x more efficient than direct top-up. There's no excuse to buy Polychrome directly without the pass.
  • Impulse pulling the last day of a banner: FOMO (fear of missing out) is designed to make you spend. If you didn't plan to pull an agent, don't panic-pull on the last day.
  • Saving too aggressively: The game is meant to be fun. If not pulling for 3 patches makes you miserable, spend your savings on a character you love — even if they're not meta.
