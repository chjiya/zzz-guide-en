🎯 ZZZ New Player Progression Roadmap — IK 1 to 60

Updated 2026-06-14 | Beginner Guide

📋 Progression Overview

Reaching Inter-Knot Level 60 is the main early-game goal in Zenless Zone Zero. This unlocks max-level content: Shiyu Defense Critical nodes, Hollow Zero Withered difficulty, and Lv. 60 Notorious Hunts. Your account's power grows through five key systems: Inter-Knot Level (account XP), Agent Level, W-Engine Level, Drive Discs, and Skill Levels.

🏁 Quick Milestones

  • IK 1-20 (Day 1-2): Main story rush, unlock all systems, build your first team
  • IK 20-35 (Week 1): Unlock Shiyu Defense, start farming promotion materials, join a faction
  • IK 35-45 (Week 2): Access S-rank Drive Disc farming (Rift 5), Hard mode story, unlock Hollow Zero Withered
  • IK 45-50 (Week 3): Unlock Shiyu Defense Critical 5, max your main DPS to Lv. 60
  • IK 50-60 (Month 1+): Max second team, optimize Drive Discs, tackle Shiyu Defense Critical 7

🗓️ Phase 1: IK 1-20 (Days 1-2)

What to Do

  • Rush Main Story: Complete Prologue through Chapter 2. Story gives the most Inter-Knot EXP per stamina spent.
  • Pick Your Starter: Choose between Soldier 11 (Fire Attack), Nekomata (Physical Attack), Grace (Electric Anomaly), Lycaon (Ice Stun), or Koleda (Fire Stun) from the standard banner S-rank. Grace and Lycaon offer the most long-term value.
  • Build Free Agents: Anby (Electric Stun), Billy (Physical Attack), and Nicole (Ether Support) are given free and remain usable even in endgame content. Prioritize Anby's dodge counter and Nicole's DEF shred.
  • Spend Battery (Stamina): Use all Battery on story commission rewards and basic material stages. Do NOT farm Drive Discs yet — you'll replace them at IK 35.
  • Video Store: Reach Video Store Manager Rank 2 by renting suggested movies daily. This unlocks significant passive Polychrome income.

💡 Day 1 Priority: Complete the tutorial → Pull 10-20 standard banner → Build Anby + Billy + Nicole → Rush Chapter 1 → Unlock Hollow Zero → Do your first Routine Cleanup for the free rewards.

Resource Management

  • Polychrome: Save ALL Polychrome for limited banners. Do NOT spend on standard banner pulls — use only Master Tapes.
  • Master Tapes: Use free Master Tapes on the standard banner until your first S-rank, then save the rest.
  • Denny: Tight at this stage. Only upgrade the agents you plan to use long-term.

🗓️ Phase 2: IK 20-35 (Week 1)

What to Do

  • Unlock Shiyu Defense: Complete the Shiyu Defense tutorial at IK 20. Even if you can't clear high floors, complete floors 1-5 for the one-time Polychrome rewards (up to 600+ Polychromes).
  • Hollow Zero Routine: Start doing Routine Cleanup weekly. Focus on clearing all investigation goals for maximum rewards. This is your main source of W-Engine upgrade materials.
  • Notorious Hunt: Begin weekly Notorious Hunt bosses once unlocked. These drop the core skill upgrade materials every agent needs.
  • Agent Ascension: Ascend your main DPS to Lv. 40. Focus resources on 1 DPS + 1 Stun + 1 Support.
  • Skill Levels: Level core passives to E (Lv. 7) and key skills to Lv. 6-7. Don't push beyond Lv. 7 until IK 45+.

🗓️ Phase 3: IK 35-45 (Week 2)

What to Do

  • Drive Disc Farming Begins: At IK 35, you unlock Rift Level 5 in the Music Store — guaranteed S-rank Drive Disc drops. Start farming for your main DPS. Do NOT use Battery on lower rifts.
  • Build a Second Team: Shiyu Defense requires two teams starting from floor 7. Build a second DPS + Support core using leftover resources. Budget options: Piper (Physical Anomaly) + Lucy (Fire Support) or Corrin (Physical Attack) + Nicole.
  • Hard Mode Story: Complete Hard Mode commissions for additional Polychrome and account EXP. These are time-limited — do them before the next patch.
  • Agent Lv. 50: Ascend your main DPS to Lv. 50. Keep supports at Lv. 40-45 to save resources.
  • W-Engine Lv. 40: Level your main DPS's W-Engine to Lv. 40. Don't go to Lv. 50+ until IK 45.

🔥 Stamina Efficiency Tip: At IK 35-45, your Battery priority should be: Drive Discs (50%) > Character Ascension Mats (25%) > Skill Mats (15%) > W-Engine Mats (10%). This balance ensures your main DPS gets decent disc sets while still progressing their level and talents.

🗓️ Phase 4: IK 45-50 (Week 3)

What to Do

  • Main DPS Lv. 60: Ascend your primary DPS to Lv. 60. This is the single biggest power spike available — it increases base ATK by ~30% and unlocks all passive talents.
  • Skill Core Passives: Push core passives to F (Lv. 9-11). These provide significant stat bonuses that compound with Drive Discs.
  • Shiyu Defense Critical: Start attempting Critical floors. Clear floor 5-7 for more Polychrome income. Focus on element-matching teams.
  • Drive Disc Optimization: Transition from "good enough" to optimized Drive Discs. Target correct main stats first, then substats.

🗓️ Phase 5: IK 50-60 (Month 1+)

What to Do

  • Full Lv. 60 Team: Bring all four core agents to Lv. 60. Stunners and support agents benefit greatly from the bulk.
  • Second Team Lv. 50-60: Raise your second team's DPS to Lv. 55-60, supports to Lv. 50.
  • Drive Disc Endgame: Farm for optimized substats: Crit Rate, Crit DMG, ATK%, PEN, Anomaly Proficiency. Target 50-70% Crit Rate + 120-160% Crit DMG on your main DPS.
  • Skill Lv. 11-12: Push core passives and key skills to Lv. 11-12. Save weekly boss drops for your most-used agents.
  • Shiyu Defense Critical 7: Full clear Shiyu Defense for maximum monthly Polychrome income (~720/month).
  • Hollow Zero Deep Dives: Clear Withered Garden advanced difficulties for namecards, resources, and achievements.

⚠️ Common New Player Mistakes

  1. Wasting Polychrome on Standard Banner: Never spend Polychrome on the standard banner. Use only free Master Tapes.
  2. Leveling Too Many Agents: Focus resources on 4-6 agents total. Spreading resources thin delays your first viable endgame team by weeks.
  3. Farming Drive Discs Before IK 35: All discs obtained before IK 35 are A-rank or worse. Wait for S-rank guaranteed drops.
  4. Ignoring Video Store: The Video Store is a passive Polychrome printer. Check it daily for movie recommendations and random events.
  5. Skipping Events: Limited-time events are the most efficient source of Polychrome, materials, and often free agents. Always prioritize event completion.
  6. Neglecting Supports: Nicole, Lucy, and Soukaku are cheap to build and dramatically improve your team's damage output. A well-built support team can double your DPS's effective output.
