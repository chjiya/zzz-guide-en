📋 Yanagi Overview & Core Mechanics

Yanagi is an S-rank Electric Anomaly agent who specializes in Disorder damage — a reaction that triggers when two different Anomaly statuses are applied to the same enemy, consuming both to deal massive burst damage. What makes Yanagi unique is that her EX Special Attack and Ultimate can trigger Disorder on command, even when the secondary anomaly hasn't fully built up yet. This gives her unparalleled control over Disorder timing compared to other anomaly agents who must wait for the anomaly gauge to fill naturally.

Yanagi's kit revolves around her Polychrome Thunder state, which activates after using her EX Special or Ultimate. In this state, her Basic Attacks are enhanced with additional Electric anomaly buildup (approximately 40% more than her normal attacks), and every 3rd enhanced attack applies a stacking Electric shock effect that increases the target's damage taken from Electric attacks by 8%, stacking up to 3 times (24% total).

Why Yanagi excels in Disorder teams: Most anomaly agents (Piper, Burnice, Jane Doe) apply their primary element and must wait for the second element's anomaly to build up naturally before Disorder procs. Yanagi's passive — Thunder's Echo — allows her EX Special to treat the target as if it has been afflicted with both elements for the purpose of Disorder calculation. This means Yanagi can trigger Disorder even if only one anomaly is fully built, effectively doubling her Disorder uptime compared to other compositions.

⚙️ How Yanagi's Disorder Works – Damage Formula

Understanding the math behind Yanagi's Disorder damage is key to building her effectively. The base Disorder damage formula is:

Disorder DMG = (Anomaly DMG of Primary Element × 0.5) + (Anomaly DMG of Secondary Element × 0.5) × (1 + 0.25 × Disorder Multiplier)

The Disorder Multiplier is increased by Yanagi's Anomaly Proficiency and certain Drive Disc bonuses. At 200 Anomaly Proficiency, the multiplier is approximately 2.5x, meaning each Disorder proc deals roughly 2.5 times the damage of a single anomaly proc.

👑 Premium Team: Yanagi + Burnice + Caesar

This is widely considered the best overall team in ZZZ's current meta for sustained damage content like Shiyu Defense. The synergy is straightforward: Burnice applies off-field Fire anomaly, Yanagi triggers Disorder on command, and Caesar provides unmatched survivability and a 1,000 ATK buff.

Performance Benchmarks (Shiyu Defense Critical Nodes): At M0 with full A-rank W-Engines: 90-110 seconds. At M0 with signature W-Engines: 65-80 seconds. At M1-M2 with signatures: 50-65 seconds. This team comfortably clears any current endgame content with S-rank time thresholds.

Why this team is so strong: Burnice's Afterburn applies a Fire DoT that ticks every 2 seconds for 10 seconds, constantly building Fire anomaly gauge. Yanagi's EX Special (which she can use every 6-8 seconds with proper Energy management) triggers Disorder on the Fire gauge while building Electric gauge simultaneously. The result is approximately 8-12 Disorder procs over a 90-second fight, each dealing 20,000-30,000 damage. Caesar's shield ensures neither Yanagi (who needs to stay on-field during Polychrome Thunder) nor Burnice (who has no self-sustain) ever get interrupted.

The long hold version applies significantly more Electric anomaly buildup and triggers a stronger Disorder. Use the held version when the enemy has a high anomaly gauge on the secondary element for maximum Disorder damage. Use the tap version for quick Disorder triggers when energy is low.

❓ FAQ

Q1: Is Yanagi worth pulling? How does she compare to other anomaly agents?

Yanagi is absolutely worth pulling if you want to play Disorder teams. She is the only character in ZZZ who can trigger Disorder on command (rather than waiting for natural buildup), which makes her the most consistent Disorder trigger in the game. Compared to Burnice (pure off-field anomaly application) or Jane Doe (pure on-field hypercarry), Yanagi offers a unique blend of on-field damage and off-field disorder synergy. If you own Caesar and/or Burnice, Yanagi is a top-tier pull.

Q2: Can Yanagi work with Jane Doe?

Yes, Yanagi + Jane Doe is a strong Disorder core. Jane Doe applies Physical anomaly rapidly through her dodge counter and enhanced Basic Attacks, while Yanagi triggers Disorder. However, both characters want significant on-field time, which creates competition. The team typically runs Jane as the primary on-field DPS and Yanagi as a quickswap Disorder bot — Yanagi swaps in → EX Special → swaps out. This team clears Shiyu Defense in approximately 100-130 seconds, slightly slower than Yanagi + Burnice but comparable.

Q3: What's Yanagi's best W-Engine if I don't have Timeweave?

Rainforest Gourmet at R5 is Yanagi's best F2P option. It provides Energy Regen (helping her maintain Disorder rotation) and ATK% scaling. Fusion Booster is a close second — it provides Anomaly Proficiency as a main stat but lacks the Energy Regen that Yanagi needs for consistent EX Special usage. Between the two, Rainforest Gourmet R5 is superior in practice because more EX Specials = more Disorders = more damage, even though Fusion Booster shows higher theoretical single-hit numbers.

Q4: What Battle Pass W-Engines should I look for?

The S-rank Battle Pass W-Engine Brimstone is a good general-purpose option for Yanagi — it provides ATK% and a stacking ATK buff on hit. However, it's not specifically designed for anomaly characters and underperforms compared to Rainforest Gourmet R5 for Disorder teams. I would not recommend buying the Battle Pass specifically for Yanagi — save your money for limited W-Engine banners instead. The A-rank Battle Pass W-Engine Styletto (Crit Rate for Attack characters) is not useful for Yanagi.
