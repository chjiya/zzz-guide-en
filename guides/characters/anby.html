Anby Demara Build Guide ⚡

📌 v1.0 • Updated June 4, 2026 • Electric • Stun • A-Rank

Overview

Anby Demara is an A-Rank Electric Stun agent and member of the Cunning Hares. As the game's free starter Stun character, Anby is the first agent every player learns to use and remains one of the most consistently valuable investments throughout the entire game. Her role is straightforward: get on the field, hit enemies with her electrified blade until their Daze meter fills, and trigger the Stun state where all incoming damage is multiplied by 150%. Unlike DPS agents whose value fluctuates with the meta, a well-built Stun agent is eternally useful because every team needs one.

Anby's kit is elegantly designed for her Stun role. Her EX Special — "Thunderbolt Slash" — is a lunging electric strike that deals high Daze damage and can be used as a gap-closer. Her Basic Attack string is fast and fluid, with the 3rd and 4th hits dealing increased Daze. What makes Anby particularly beginner-friendly is her Dodge Counter: it applies a large burst of Daze and has generous i-frames, letting newer players play defensively while still contributing to the Stun meter. Her Additional Ability grants bonus Daze against Shocked enemies, giving her natural synergy with Electric-element teammates.

The key to mastering Anby is understanding that she's not a damage dealer. Her personal damage output is low, and investing in ATK or CRIT on her is a trap. Every stat on Anby should serve one purpose: filling the Daze meter faster. Impact is her god stat — it directly scales the Daze value of every attack. Energy Regen lets her use high-Daze EX Specials more often. That's it. A correctly built Anby triggers Stun 30-40% faster than one built for damage, and those faster Stun windows translate directly into faster clear times for your DPS.

Main Stat Recommendations

  • Disc 4: CRIT Rate (for triggering Woodpecker effects) or ATK% — Impact is not available on Disc 4, so pick stats that provide minor utility. CRIT Rate is preferred.
  • Disc 5: Electric DMG Bonus or ATK% — Electric DMG if you have Electric DPS teammates for Shock application; ATK% otherwise.
  • Disc 6: Impact — THIS IS NON-NEGOTIABLE. Impact directly scales the Daze value of every attack Anby makes. There is no alternative main stat worth considering on Disc 6.

Substat Priority

  1. Impact — only available on Disc 6 main stat; cannot roll as a substat
  2. Energy Regen — more EX Specials means faster Stun triggers
  3. ATK% — minor but helps Anby's personal damage slightly
  4. CRIT Rate — low priority; only for triggering conditional disc effects

Best Team Compositions

F2P Starter Team (Universal)

  • Any Physical/Electric DPS — Corin, Billy, or Anton
  • Anby (Stun) — Primary Daze builder, sets up Stun windows
  • Nicole (Support) — DEF shred, black hole grouping, free starter agent

This is the most accessible team in ZZZ and can clear all story content plus early Shiyu Defense. Anby builds Daze → Nicole debuffs → DPS bursts during Stun. The core is so solid that it works with any Physical or Electric DPS you have available.

Electric Synergy Team

  • Anton (DPS) — Electric burst damage during Stun windows
  • Anby (Stun) — Electric element activates Additional Abilities for both agents. Bonus Daze against Shocked enemies
  • Grace or Rina (Support) — Grace provides Shock for Anby's passive; Rina provides PEN Ratio and Shock extension

Premium Universal Slot

  • Any S-Rank DPS — Miyabi, Ellen Joe, Zhu Yuan, Soldier 11
  • Anby (Stun) — Reliable Stun application regardless of element
  • Any Support — Soukaku, Rina, Caesar, or Nicole depending on DPS element

Anby's greatest strength is her universality. Unlike Lycaon (Ice-locked) or Koleda (Fire-synergistic), Anby slots into any team and performs consistently. While she won't provide elemental RES shred like premium Stun agents, her raw Daze output keeps her competitive as a substitute in any composition.

Combat Rotation & Tips

  1. Opener: Start with Anby on-field. Use EX Special (Thunderbolt Slash) to close distance and immediately start building Daze. The lunge is also a gap-closer.
  2. Daze Phase: Complete Anby's full 5-hit Basic Attack string repeatedly. The 3rd and 4th hits deal bonus Daze — do not cancel the string early. Weave in Dodge Counters for extra Daze while avoiding damage.
  3. Energy Usage: Use EX Special whenever you have enough Energy and the enemy isn't already near full Daze. Wasting EX Special on an enemy 90% dazed is inefficient.
  4. Pre-Stun Buff: When the Daze meter is at 90%, swap to your Support to apply buffs/debuffs, then swap back to Anby to land the final hits and trigger Stun.
  5. Stun Window: As soon as Stun triggers, Chain Attack into your DPS. Do not let Anby take the Chain Attack unless you have no other option — her damage contribution is minimal.
  6. Repeat: After the Stun window ends, swap back to Anby and begin the cycle again. Her Daze output is consistent throughout the fight with no ramp-up required.

Key Tips: Anby's EX Special has two charges — you can fire it twice back-to-back for massive burst Daze. Save the double-EX for when you need to push the Daze meter from 50% to Stun quickly. Don't neglect Dodge Counters: Anby's is one of the fastest and safest in the game, and it applies more Daze than two Basic Attacks. In multi-enemy scenarios, her Basic Attack string has moderate AoE — position to hit as many enemies as possible.

Mindscape Cinema (Duplicates) Value

  • M1: EX Special gains 15% increased Daze. ~12% faster Stun application. Excellent and easily achievable.
  • M2: Core Passive Impact bonus increased by 20%. Direct Impact boost translates to ~10% faster Stun on all attacks. Strong passive upgrade.
  • M4: Dodging successfully restores 5 Energy. Rewards active dodge-heavy play with more EX Special usage. ~15% more Energy over a fight.
  • M6: Enemies stunned by Anby take 12% increased damage for the Stun duration. This is Anby's crown jewel — effectively raises the Stun damage multiplier from 150% to 168% for the entire team. Massive team DPS increase.

Anby's M1 and M2 are easily achievable as a launch A-Rank and provide meaningful upgrades. M6 is transformative, giving Anby a unique team-wide damage amplification that no other A-Rank Stun agent provides. Long-term, M6 Anby is competitive with M0 premium Stun agents for non-elemental teams.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best W-Engine for Anby?

Anby's best W-Engine is The Restrained, providing Impact as its main stat and increasing Daze dealt to enemies — every stat directly accelerates her Stun speed. The craftable Steam Oven is the best F2P option with Energy Regen that lets Anby use her high-Daze EX Special more frequently, resulting in faster and more consistent Stun windows.

Q: Which Drive Discs should Anby use?

The optimal setup is 4-piece Shockstar Disco and 2-piece Swing Jazz. Shockstar Disco provides Impact as its 2-piece bonus and 20% increased Daze as its 4-piece effect — this is the definitive Stun agent disc set. Swing Jazz provides 20% Energy Regen, letting Anby use her high-Daze EX Special significantly more often for faster Stun triggers.

Q: What are Anby's best teammates?

As a universal Stun agent, Anby fits into virtually any DPS-Stun-Support team. She pairs especially well with Nicole and any Physical or Electric DPS (Corin, Billy, Anton) for a complete F2P core. Her Additional Ability provides bonus Daze against Shocked enemies, making Electric DPS teammates like Anton particularly synergistic for maximum Daze output and faster clear times.

Q: Should I build ATK or Impact on Anby?

Always prioritize Impact over ATK on Anby. Impact directly scales the Daze value of every attack she makes — it is the single stat that determines how fast you trigger Stun windows for your DPS. Building ATK on Anby is a trap; her personal damage is negligible regardless of investment. Impact on Disc 6 is mandatory, and Impact from W-Engine and Drive Discs is your highest priority.

Q: Is Anby worth investing in long-term?

Yes, Anby is one of the safest investments in ZZZ. As a Stun agent, her value is tied to her utility (triggering Stun windows), not her personal damage output. She never falls off because every team needs a Stun agent, and she remains viable as a substitute even after you acquire premium S-Rank Stun agents like Lycaon or Qingyi for elemental-specific teams.

Q: How does Anby compare to premium Stun agents?

Anby has the fastest and safest Dodge Counter among all Stun agents, making her more forgiving for newer players. However, Lycaon and Qingyi provide elemental RES shred which amplifies team damage beyond raw Daze output. Anby's M6 (12% increased damage against stunned enemies) partially bridges this gap, making a fully invested Anby competitive with M0 premium options for non-elemental teams.
