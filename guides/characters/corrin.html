Corin Wickes Build Guide ⚙️

📌 v1.0 • Updated June 4, 2026 • Physical • Attack • A-Rank

Overview

Corin Wickes is an A-Rank Physical Attack agent and member of Victoria Housekeeping Co. Despite her timid personality, Corin wields a massive motorized chainsaw that delivers some of the highest single-target burst damage among A-Rank characters. Her kit revolves around her EX Special: Corin revs up her chainsaw and unleashes a continuous stream of Physical damage that hits 10+ times over several seconds. While channeling, she gains super armor and damage reduction, letting her face-tank through enemy attacks while tearing through their HP bars.

Corin's primary strength lies in Stun window burst damage. When an enemy is stunned (taking 150% increased damage), Corin's fully channeled EX Special can deal damage comparable to S-Rank DPS units. Her Chain Attack also deals significant damage and transitions smoothly into her EX Special, creating a seamless burst rotation. As a free character available to all players, Corin is one of the best starter DPS options and remains viable through mid-to-late game content with proper investment.

The main limitation to Corin's kit is Energy management. Her EX Special consumes Energy rapidly — at maximum channel, it can drain her entire Energy bar. This means Corin has defined burst windows followed by downtime where she relies on Basic Attacks. Learning to time her EX Special so that the full channel completes during the Stun window is the key skill that separates average Corin players from optimized ones.

Main Stat Recommendations

  • Disc 4: CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG — aim for 1:2 ratio. CRIT Rate recommended as baseline since Corin's many small hits benefit from consistent crits.
  • Disc 5: Physical DMG Bonus — amplifies all of Corin's damage since she deals exclusively Physical damage. No alternatives.
  • Disc 6: ATK% — Corin has no Energy or special scaling needs. Pure ATK% provides the largest consistent damage gain.

Substat Priority

  1. CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG — aim for 50%+ CRIT Rate and 100%+ CRIT DMG
  2. ATK% — every piece should ideally have ATK% as a substat
  3. Flat ATK — additive but better than defensive stats
  4. Energy Regen — helps reduce downtime between burst windows

Combat Rotation & Tips

  1. Downtime Phase: Keep Corin off-field. Use your Stun agent to build Daze. Corin's Basic Attacks are weak compared to her burst — she should only be on-field during Stun windows.
  2. Pre-Stun Setup: When the Daze meter is near full, use Nicole's EX Special or your Support's buff skill. You want buffs active when the Stun window opens.
  3. Stun Window Opener: Chain Attack into Corin as the third Chain — this maximizes the Chain Attack's multiplier bonus (each successive Chain Attack deals more damage).
  4. Burst Rotation: Immediately after Chain Attack lands, hold EX Special and channel for the full duration. Do not release early — the final hit deals the most damage.
  5. Ultimate Usage: Use Corin's Ultimate at the end of the EX Special channel, just before the Stun window closes. This squeezes maximum damage from the 150% Stun multiplier.
  6. Energy Recovery: After burst, swap back to Stun agent. Corin should not stay on-field without Energy for EX Special.

Key Tips: Always enter a boss fight with full Energy on Corin. Her EX Special provides damage reduction (not invincibility), so she can still die if you face-tank a boss's heavy attack. Learn which attacks to dodge and which to tank through. Nicole's black hole grouping is crucial — Corin's chainsaw has a moderate AoE that hits all enemies pulled together, multiplying her effective damage.

Mindscape Cinema (Duplicates) Value

  • M1: EX Special final hit deals 40% increased damage. ~20% burst damage increase. Highly recommended and easily achievable.
  • M2: Chain Attack reduces EX Special Energy cost by 30% for the next use. Improves Energy economy significantly. ~15% more uptime.
  • M4: Core Passive ATK stacks increase from 5 to 8 maximum. ~18% sustained ATK increase during long fights.
  • M6: EX Special hits have 15% chance to deal double damage. With 10+ hits, this consistently procs 1-2 times per channel. ~25% total burst damage increase. Transformative.

Corin has an excellent M2 breakpoint that partially solves her Energy issues. M6 is a substantial power spike but achievable over time since she appears on all banners. Even at M0, Corin performs well as a budget DPS for early-to-mid game content.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best W-Engine for Corin?

Corin's best W-Engine is The Brimstone, providing ATK% and a stacking damage buff on hit that ramps up during her extended EX Special chainsaw. For F2P players, Starlight Engine is excellent — its ATK buff triggers on Dodge Counter which Corin uses frequently to reposition between chainsaw burst windows.

Q: Which Drive Discs should Corin use?

The optimal setup is 4-piece Fanged Metal and 2-piece Woodpecker Electro. Fanged Metal provides Physical DMG Bonus and a stacking ATK buff on Assault triggers, while Woodpecker Electro's 8% CRIT Rate is crucial since Corin's chainsaw EX Special deals many small hits that benefit significantly from consistent critical strikes.

Q: What are Corin's best teammates?

Corin works best with Anby (free Stun agent) and Nicole (free Support) for a complete F2P team. Nicole's black hole groups enemies perfectly for Corin's AoE chainsaw, while Anby provides fast Daze for Stun windows. Lucy is a premium alternative for team-wide ATK buffs and faction synergy.

Q: How do I manage Corin's Energy properly?

Only use Corin's EX Special during Stun windows — never use it outside of them. This ensures every point of Energy is spent dealing 150% amplified damage. Between burst windows, keep Corin off-field entirely and let your Stun agent do all the work. Aim to enter every boss fight with a full Energy bar for optimal burst output.

Q: Is Corin worth building over other A-Rank DPS?

Yes, Corin is one of the best A-Rank DPS investments. She outdamages Billy against single targets and has a lower skill floor than characters requiring specific conditions. Her free availability and natural Cinema progression make her the most reliable F2P carry for story content, early Shiyu Defense floors, and material farming stages.

Q: Should I use ATK% or Physical DMG on Disc 5?

Always use Physical DMG Bonus on Disc 5. Physical DMG Bonus occupies a separate multiplier bucket from ATK%, meaning the two stats multiply each other rather than adding together. Even with high ATK% from substats and buffs, Physical DMG Bonus provides significantly more total damage because it amplifies your entire damage formula rather than just the ATK component.
