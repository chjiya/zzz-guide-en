Ellen Joe Build Guide 🦈

📌 v1.0 • Updated June 4, 2026 • Ice • Attack • S-Rank

Overview

Ellen Joe is an S-Rank Ice Attack agent and a fan-favorite character from Victoria Housekeeping Co. Known affectionately as the "shark maid" by the community, Ellen wields a massive scissors-like weapon and delivers devastating Ice damage through her unique Flash Freeze mechanic. Her playstyle rewards aggressive, high-APM gameplay where she builds Flash Freeze charges by executing consecutive Basic Attacks and Dodge Counters, then consumes those charges during her EX Special or Ultimate for massive amplified damage.

As one of the earliest limited banner characters released in ZZZ, Ellen Joe established herself as the gold standard for Ice DPS from day one. Her burst damage potential during Stun windows is among the highest in the game, and when paired with her ideal teammates (Lycaon and Soukaku), she forms the core of what many players consider the strongest mono-element team in ZZZ. Her Flash Freeze mechanic means she gets progressively stronger during combat, with each charge increasing her next ice attack's damage by a multiplicative percentage.

Ellen's Additional Ability increases her Ice DMG by 20% when her HP is above 80%, which means she rewards skilled play and careful positioning. Combined with her innate mobility from her Dodge Attack (which has extended i-frames compared to most agents), Ellen is a high-skill-ceiling DPS that rewards mastery. For players who enjoy fast-paced, combo-intensive gameplay and want a DPS that scales excellently with player skill, Ellen Joe is an outstanding investment that remains competitive in all current endgame modes.

Main Stat Recommendations

  • Disc 4: CRIT Rate — Ellen needs consistency for her Flash Freeze burst. Missing a crit on her charged EX Special is a massive DPS loss. Aim for 60%+ CRIT Rate.
  • Disc 5: Ice DMG Bonus — non-negotiable. Ellen's entire kit scales on Ice damage.
  • Disc 6: ATK% — standard DPS choice. Ellen has no special scaling needs that would justify Energy Regen or other stats.

Best Team Compositions

Premium Mono-Ice (Ellen's Dream Team)

  • Ellen Joe (DPS) — Primary on-field carry
  • Von Lycaon (Stun) — Applies Ice RES shred on stunned enemies (-25%) and builds Daze rapidly with charged EX Specials
  • Soukaku (Support) — Massive ATK buff (up to 1000+ ATK at max level) via her Vortex → Fly the Flag mechanic, plus additional Ice DMG from her Core Passive

This trio represents one of the most synergistic team compositions in all of ZZZ. Lycaon's Ice RES shred makes Ellen's burst hit significantly harder, while Soukaku's ATK steroid — which can reach upwards of 1000 flat ATK — is essentially a second W-Engine worth of stats. The rotation is smooth: Soukaku builds Vortex → Lycaon builds Daze → Soukaku uses Fly the Flag for the ATK buff → Chain Attack into Ellen → EX Special at max Flash Freeze charges for a devastating finisher. This team consistently achieves the fastest Shiyu Defense clears among Ice-element compositions.

Combat Rotation & Tips

  1. Charge Building Phase: Start the fight with Ellen on-field. Use her full 4-hit Basic Attack string repeatedly to build Flash Freeze charges. Each completed combo generates 2 charges, and you can hold up to 6 charges maximum.
  2. Soukaku Setup: When Soukaku's Energy is full, swap to her and use her EX Special (2 charges) to build Vortex stacks. Once at 3 Vortex stacks, hold her Special Attack button to trigger Fly the Flag, which consumes Vortex and grants the team a massive ATK buff for 20 seconds.
  3. Lycaon Daze Phase: With the ATK buff active, swap to Lycaon and aggressively build Daze using his charged EX Special and Basic combo. Lycaon's charged Basic (hold) applies significantly more Daze.
  4. Burst Execution: When the enemy enters Stun state: Perfect Assist into Ellen → use her EX Special (consumes all Flash Freeze charges at max multiplier) → if Ultimate is available, use it immediately after EX Special for a second burst of amplified Ice damage. At 6 charges, this burst sequence can deal over 200k damage in a single Stun window.
  5. HP Management: Ellen's Core Passive requires HP above 80% for the full 20% Ice DMG Bonus. Play carefully during the Daze-building phase — if you take damage, consider using Soukaku's healing (if unlocked at higher Cinema levels) or play more conservatively until you can heal.

Advanced Techniques: Ellen's 3rd Basic Attack has a long animation that can be animation-canceled with EX Special for faster charge cycling. Her Dodge Attack has extended i-frames compared to other agents — use this proactively to dodge through boss attacks while maintaining offensive pressure. The Flash Freeze charge indicator (small ice crystals orbiting Ellen) is visible during combat; learn to track it at a glance so you always know when to burst.

Mindscape Cinema Value

  • M1: Flash Freeze max charges increased from 6 to 8, and each charge now provides 2% CRIT DMG. Roughly 20% total damage increase. Highly recommended.
  • M2: EX Special grants 2 additional Flash Freeze charges after use, smoothing out the charge economy for back-to-back burst windows. ~15% DPS gain through consistency.
  • M4: Core Passive HP threshold lowered from 80% to 50%, making the 20% Ice DMG Bonus much easier to maintain. Excellent quality-of-life upgrade.
  • M6: Flash Freeze charges now also apply to Basic Attacks (not just EX Special and Ultimate), fundamentally changing Ellen into a sustained hypercarry. Transforms her playstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Ellen Joe's best W-Engine?

Deep Sea Visitor is Ellen's signature W-Engine and her best-in-slot, providing Ice DMG Bonus and increased CRIT Rate against Frozen enemies. For F2P players, the craftable Starlight Engine and Battle Pass Cannon Rotor are both excellent alternatives that allow Ellen to perform competitively in all content.

Q: What Drive Discs should Ellen use?

The recommended setup is 4-piece Polar Metal and 2-piece Woodpecker Electro. Polar Metal provides Ice DMG Bonus and ATK after EX Special or Chain Attack, directly synergizing with Ellen's burst rotation. Woodpecker Electro's CRIT Rate helps ensure consistent Flash Freeze burst damage.

Q: Who synergizes best with Ellen Joe?

Ellen's best teammates are Lycaon (Ice Stun with 25% Ice RES shred on stunned enemies) and Soukaku (Ice Support with massive ATK buffs via Vortex mechanic). This mono-Ice core provides full elemental synergy and covers all essential team roles — DPS, Stun, and Support — making it one of the strongest predefined teams in ZZZ.

Q: How do I play Ellen Joe effectively?

The core loop is: build Flash Freeze charges with Basic Attacks → have Soukaku provide ATK buffs → use Lycaon to Stun the enemy → swap to Ellen and unleash EX Special at max charges during the Stun window. Managing Flash Freeze charges and timing your burst is the key to maximizing her damage output.

Q: Is Ellen Joe still good in the current meta?

Yes, Ellen remains one of the strongest Ice DPS agents. While Miyabi offers higher theoretical damage ceilings, Ellen's consistent performance, excellent F2P options, and the strength of her mono-Ice team with Lycaon and Soukaku keep her firmly in the S-tier for Ice DPS. She excels particularly in Shiyu Defense stages with Ice-weak enemies.

Q: Should I pull for Ellen's Mindscape Cinema or her W-Engine first?

Always prioritize her signature W-Engine (Deep Sea Visitor) before Mindscape Cinema. The W-Engine provides larger percentage damage gains and can be shared with other Ice DPS agents if needed. M1 is the next best investment if you are a dedicated Ellen main.
