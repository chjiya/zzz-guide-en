TL;DR

Koleda is an S-rank Fire Stun agent and the president of Belobog Industries. She wields a massive hammer and specializes in fast Daze buildup through heavy EX Special attacks and chain attack setups. Her Belobog faction passives make her the ideal stunner for Soldier 11 and other Belobog DPS units. She offers fast stuns with excellent Chain Attack multiplier support.

Core Mechanics

Hammer Stun Combos

Koleda's basic attack string uses her hammer in sweeping arcs that build consistent Daze. Her third hit has a particularly high Daze multiplier, making animation-cancelling into Dodge Counter a common optimization. Her EX Special Attack is her primary stun tool, delivering massive single-hit Daze damage.

Chain Attack Amplifier

Koleda's passive increases Chain Attack damage dealt by the entire team when she triggers a stun. This makes her exceptionally good in burst-oriented teams that rely on Chain Attack rotations for their damage profile, such as Belobog Industrial teams where multiple members benefit from chain attacks.

Stat Priority

  • Disc IV: HP% / DEF% (survivability for close-range play)
  • Disc V: Energy Regen
  • Disc VI: Impact% (mandatory)
  • Substats: Impact% > Energy Regen > ATK% > HP%

Skill Priority

  1. Special Attack (EX): Primary Daze source and gap closer
  2. Chain Attack: Huge burst Daze and team setup
  3. Basic Attack: Third hit Daze is significant
  4. Dodge: Dodge Counter maintains Daze pressure
  5. Assist: Lowest priority, minimal stun contribution

Best Team Compositions

Premium: Koleda + Soldier 11 + Lucy

The signature Belobog Fire team. Soldier 11's consistent Fire damage output during stun windows is perfectly complemented by Koleda's fast stuns and Chain Attack amp. Lucy provides ATK buffs and off-field damage with her piggy support.

Belobog: Koleda + Ben + Grace

Pure Belobog synergy team. Ben provides shields and synergizes with Koleda's faction passives, while Grace (or Anton) serves as the Electric DPS for coverage. This team is tanky and consistent but lacks top-end damage.

Flex Fire: Koleda + Burnice + Lucy

Burnice handles off-field Fire anomaly while Koleda stuns and Lucy buffers. This team relies on Koleda's stun windows for Burnice's Disorder procs, creating a hybrid stun-burn playstyle.

Mindscape Cinema Priority

  • M1: Increased Daze on chain attack — noticeable stun speed improvement
  • M2: Team-wide Energy on stun — great for rotation support
  • M4: Self ATK buff after EX — minor personal damage increase
  • M6: Major damage — Chain Attacks inflict bonus damage

Rotation Tips

Optimal opener: EX Special Attack to close distance → Basic combo through third hit → Dodge Counter → EX Special again when energy is available. In stun windows, always use Koleda's Chain Attack first to buff the subsequent DPS Chain Attacks.

Koleda's short range can be punishing against mobile bosses. Learning enemy attack patterns to land consistent EX Special hits is crucial. Equip at least one survival substat to avoid being one-shot during close-range trades.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Koleda better than Lighter?

Lighter is generally stronger due to his Blaze Knuckle self-buffing and better team support passives. However, Koleda remains excellent and specifically outshines Lighter in Belobog faction teams with Soldier 11 or Ben.

Can Koleda work outside Fire teams?

Yes. She functions as a general-purpose stunner due to her fast Daze buildup. She loses her faction passives but still performs well with any DPS that benefits from quick stun rotations.

What Bangboo should I use with Koleda?

Safety (S-rank) for Belobog teams. Rocketboo for generic Fire teams. Avocaboo for sustain if your team lacks healing.

Do I need Impact% on Disc VI?

Yes. Impact% is non-negotiable for stunners. Without it, your stun speed drops dramatically. Every substat roll into Impact% is valuable.
