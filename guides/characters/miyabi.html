Hoshimi Miyabi Build Guide ❄️

📌 v1.0 • Updated June 4, 2026 • Ice • Attack • S-Rank

Overview

Hoshimi Miyabi is an S-Rank Ice Attack agent and stands as one of the most devastating DPS characters in Zenless Zone Zero. As the Chief of Hollow Special Operations Section 6, Miyabi wields a katana with surgical precision and delivers massive Ice damage through her unique Frostbite stacking mechanic. Her kit revolves around building Frostbite stacks on enemies via Basic Attacks and EX Specials, then consuming those stacks with her enhanced Charged Attack to unleash a series of rapid slashes that deal devastating burst damage.

Miyabi excels in both sustained DPS and burst windows. Her Frostbite mechanic means she gets stronger as fights go longer — each stack increases the damage of her next Charged Attack finisher, and at maximum stacks (8), her fully charged attack hits with AoE Ice damage that can clear entire waves of enemies. In Shiyu Defense and Hollow Zero, Miyabi consistently ranks among the top-performing DPS units thanks to her high multipliers, self-buffing capabilities, and synergistic teammates that can amplify her Ice output.

What makes Miyabi particularly valuable is her flexibility. While she naturally shines in mono-Ice teams alongside Lycaon and Soukaku, she also performs exceptionally well with universal supports like Rina or Caesar. Her Additional Ability grants her a stacking ATK bonus every time she triggers a Frostbite Shatter effect, making her increasingly dangerous over the course of a fight. For players looking to invest in a top-tier DPS that will remain relevant across multiple patches, Miyabi is an outstanding choice.

Skill Priority

When leveling Miyabi's skills, follow this priority order to maximize her damage output efficiently. Her core damage comes from her Charged Attack (Frostbite Shatter) and Basic Attack chain, so these take top priority:

1stBasic Attack: ShinotsukuPrimary damage source; all Frostbite stacks are generated through her 5-hit Basic string. Each level increases the base multiplier and Frostbite generation rate.
2ndEX Special: ZanshinGenerates 2 Frostbite stacks on hit, provides i-frames, and deals significant damage. Also procs her Additional ATK buff.
3rdChain AttackUsed frequently in Stun windows; high multipliers and applies Frostbite on the final hit.
4thUltimate: SetsugekkaMassive single-target burst that applies max Frostbite stacks. Leveling this yields high burst damage returns.
5thDodge AttackNice to have but not priority; the Dodge Counter applies 1 Frostbite stack.
6thCore PassiveImportant for the ATK scaling passive at max level, but unlock levels are gated by Agent rank.

Best Drive Discs

Miyabi's Drive Disc setup is straightforward: she wants Ice DMG amplification, CRIT stats, and ATK. The 4-piece Polar Metal set is non-negotiable — it is purpose-built for Ice DPS agents and synergizes perfectly with her kit. Here are the optimal configurations:

SetupDiscsBest For
Optimal4-Polar Metal + 2-Woodpecker ElectroGeneral purpose best setup. Polar Metal's 2-piece gives 10% Ice DMG Bonus; 4-piece grants 20% ATK for 12s after using an EX Special or landing a Chain Attack. Woodpecker Electro provides 8% CRIT Rate. This is the recommended setup for 99% of situations.
High CRIT4-Polar Metal + 2-Branch & Blade SongUse this if you already have 50%+ CRIT Rate from substats. Branch & Blade Song provides 16% CRIT DMG, pushing your crit multiplier higher at the cost of consistency.
Budget4-Polar Metal + 2-Hormone PunkAlternative if you lack good Woodpecker pieces. Hormone Punk's 2-piece gives 10% ATK, which is a serviceable stat stick while farming better discs.

Main Stat Recommendations

  • Disc 4: CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG — aim for a 1:2 ratio. If using Hailstorm Shrine, go CRIT DMG. Otherwise, CRIT Rate is safer.
  • Disc 5: Ice DMG Bonus — no alternatives. This is Miyabi's primary damage type and the biggest multiplier source.
  • Disc 6: ATK% — Miyabi scales purely off ATK and has no Energy or anomaly needs. ATK% provides the largest consistent damage gain.

Substat Priority

  1. CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG — aim for 60%+ CRIT Rate and 120%+ CRIT DMG as baseline targets
  2. ATK% — every piece should ideally have ATK% as a substat
  3. Flat ATK — small but additive; better than DEF or HP
  4. PEN — niche, only relevant against high-DEF enemies in Shiyu Defense floor 10+

Best Team Compositions

Miyabi works best in teams that can apply frequent Stun, debuff enemies with Ice RES reduction, or provide direct ATK and DMG buffs. Below are her three strongest team archetypes:

Premium Mono-Ice Team

  • Miyabi (DPS) — Main on-field damage dealer
  • Lycaon (Stun) — Builds Daze quickly, applies Ice RES shred on stunned enemies
  • Soukaku (Support) — Provides massive ATK buffs via her Vortex mechanic and additional Ice DMG amplification

Rotation: Start with Soukaku to build Vortex stacks → swap to Lycaon for Daze buildup → when enemy is stunned, Soukaku's ATK buff → Chain into Miyabi → use EX Special → fully charged Basic for Frostbite Shatter. This rotation consistently delivers 150k+ damage per burst window at high investment.

Budget-Friendly Team

  • Miyabi (DPS)
  • Anby (Stun) — Free Stun agent, reliable Daze application
  • Soukaku or Nicole (Support) — Soukaku for ATK buffs, Nicole for DEF shred and grouping

Flex Team (Non-Ice Synergy)

  • Miyabi (DPS)
  • Rina (Support) — PEN Ratio buffs help Miyabi punch through enemy defenses
  • Caesar (Defense/Stun) — Shield, ATK buff, and Daze application in one slot. Extremely safe and consistent.

Combat Rotation & Tips

To extract maximum value from Miyabi, you need to understand her Frostbite stacking rhythm. Here is the optimal in-combat flow:

  1. Opener: Use Soukaku's EX Special to start building Vortex stacks and buffing the team's ATK. Cancel into Lycaon or your Stun agent.
  2. Daze Phase: With your Stun agent, apply consistent pressure with Basic Attacks and EX Specials. The goal is to push the enemy into a Stun state as quickly as possible.
  3. Burst Window: Once the enemy is stunned, immediately trigger Soukaku's Fly the Flag (if using her) for the ATK buff. Perfect Assist or Quick Assist into Miyabi.
  4. Miyabi Combo: EX Special → Basic Attack string (full 5 hits) → Hold Basic Attack for Frostbite Shatter. At 8 stacks, the fully charged attack hits for up to 800% of ATK as Ice damage.
  5. Ultimate Usage: Use Miyabi's Ultimate after triggering the Chain Attack but before the Frostbite Shatter. The Ultimate applies max Frostbite stacks instantly, letting you immediately follow with a fully charged attack.
  6. Repeat: After the burst, swap back to your Stun agent and begin building Daze again. Miyabi's Frostbite stacks persist between swaps, so you can bank them for the next burst window.

Key Tips: Always animation cancel Miyabi's 4th Basic Attack into her EX Special — this saves frames and keeps your combo fluid. Her Dodge Counter is surprisingly strong and should be used offensively, not just defensively. In multi-wave content like Hollow Zero, try to carry Frostbite stacks between waves by holding your Charged Attack until the next group spawns.

Mindscape Cinema (Duplicates) Value

Miyabi's Mindscape Cinema levels (M1-M6) provide significant power spikes at key breakpoints:

  • M1: Increases Frostbite max stacks from 8 to 10 and grants 10% Ice RES PEN on Frostbite Shatter. ~15% damage increase. Highly recommended.
  • M2: Charged Attack generates 2 additional Energy for the next EX Special. Improves rotation fluidity significantly. ~10% DPS gain.
  • M4: Ultimate damage increased by 30% and reduces Ultimate cooldown. Niche but powerful for speedrunning.
  • M6: Every 3rd Frostbite Shatter deals 100% increased damage and applies Freeze. Transformative; turns Miyabi into the undisputed best DPS in the game.

For most players, stopping at M1 provides the best cost-to-power ratio. Only pursue M6 if you are fully committed to maining Miyabi long-term.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best W-Engine for Hoshimi Miyabi?

Hoshimi Miyabi's signature W-Engine, Hailstorm Shrine, is her best-in-slot option providing massive Ice DMG Bonus and CRIT stats with a Charged Attack amplification effect. The Battle Pass W-Engine Cannon Rotor and the craftable Starlight Engine are excellent F2P alternatives that still perform competitively.

Q: Which Drive Discs should Miyabi use?

The optimal setup is 4-piece Polar Metal and 2-piece Woodpecker Electro. Polar Metal grants Ice DMG Bonus and a substantial ATK buff after using an EX Special or Chain Attack, while Woodpecker Electro provides 8% CRIT Rate which is crucial for her crit-scaling kit.

Q: What are Miyabi's best teammates?

Miyabi pairs excellently with Lycaon (Ice Stun) and Soukaku (Ice Support) for a mono-Ice comp. Alternatively, she works well with Rina for PEN Ratio buffs or with Caesar for shields and ATK buffs. Any agent that provides ATK buffs, Ice RES shred, or fast Daze application is an excellent partner.

Q: How much CRIT Rate does Miyabi need?

Aim for at least 60% CRIT Rate as a baseline, with an ideal target of 70-75%. Because Miyabi's damage comes in fewer, larger hits (especially her Frostbite Shatter), missing a crit on her Charged Attack is a significant DPS loss. CRIT DMG should be at double your CRIT Rate for optimal ratio.

Q: Is Miyabi worth pulling as a new player?

Absolutely. Miyabi is one of the strongest and most self-sufficient DPS agents in the game. She performs well even with F2P gear and accessible teammates like Anby and Soukaku. Her Ice element is universally strong across current content, and she scales exceptionally well with investment.

Q: Should I use ATK% or Ice DMG on Disc 5?

Always use Ice DMG Bonus on Disc 5. The damage formula in ZZZ heavily favors Elemental DMG Bonus over ATK% because they occupy different multiplier buckets. Ice DMG Bonus is a separate multiplier that provides significantly more total damage than an equivalent amount of ATK%.
