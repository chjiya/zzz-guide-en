📋 Character Overview

Nicole Demara is an A-rank Ether Support character in Zenless Zone Zero and the charismatic leader of the Cunning Hares. She wields an Ether-powered hand cannon that generates gravity fields on the battlefield, providing group DEF shred, enemy crowd control, and Ether damage amplification. Nicole is widely considered one of the most versatile and valuable support units in the game — her DEF shred debuff benefits every team composition, and her gravity well is indispensable for mob-clearing content.

Nicole's core value comes from her Special Attack: Ether Gravity Field, which creates a zone that pulls enemies together and reduces their DEF by a flat percentage. This field lasts for 8 seconds and has a 12-second cooldown, meaning with enough Energy Regen she can maintain nearly 100% uptime. Her Ultimate unleashes a massive Ether explosion that also pulls in nearby enemies and applies a stronger DEF shred. The beauty of Nicole's kit is that she requires almost no field time — cast her skill, swap to your DPS, and enjoy amplified damage.

Strengths: Universal DEF shred works with any damage type; gravity pull solves enemy scattering; free-to-play friendly (given at start); low field-time requirement makes her a true quick-swap support. Weaknesses: Extremely low personal damage; entirely dependent on teammates for effectiveness; Energy Regen requirements are strict to maintain optimal uptime; lacks healing or shielding utility.

💿 Drive Disc Recommendations

Best Set: Swing Jazz (4-Piece) + Chaotic Metal (2-Piece)

Swing Jazz 4-Piece: Increases Energy Regen by 20% — this is the most important stat for Nicole. Additionally, after using her Ultimate, the entire team gains 15% increased damage for 12 seconds. Since Nicole generates Energy quickly and her Ultimate has relatively low cost, this buff has very high uptime in prolonged fights.

Chaotic Metal 2-Piece: Increases Ether DMG by 10%. While Nicole's personal damage isn't her selling point, the extra Ether damage helps with cleanup and anomaly application, especially in Ether-focused team comps with Zhu Yuan.

Alternate Set: Swing Jazz (4-Piece) + Chaotic Metal (2-Piece)

This is effectively her only set. Other support sets like Protopunk (shield-based) don't work with Nicole's kit. The Swing Jazz 4-piece is non-negotiable for maximizing her Energy Regen and team buff potential.

🤝 Team Compositions

Nicole's role is support / DEF shred enabler. She amplifies the entire team's damage regardless of element, making her one of the most flexible characters in ZZZ. Here are the top team compositions:

🥇 Ether Hypercarry (Best)

Zhu Yuan + Qingyi + Nicole

This is a Tier-0 team in the current meta. Zhu Yuan is the Ether Attack DPS with massive burst potential. Qingyi provides the fastest stun application in the game, creating extended damage windows. Nicole's DEF shred amplifies Zhu Yuan's Ether damage by roughly 22-25%, and the Ether resonance between Zhu Yuan and Nicole boosts team Ether DMG by an additional 15%. Rotation: Qingyi stuns → Nicole E to shred DEF → Zhu Yuan chain attack → full combo. This team clears Shiyu Defense floors 15+ with ease when properly built.

🥈 Cunning Hares Classic (F2P)

Billy + Anby + Nicole

The free starter team that carries you through the entire story and early endgame. Billy's Physical Attack damage benefits enormously from Nicole's DEF shred (about 20% effective DPS increase). Anby provides reliable A-rank stun application. Nicole's gravity field solves Billy's weakness against scattered enemies by clumping them together for his piercing shots. This team costs zero Polychrome to build and clears all story content comfortably.

🥉 Universal Support Slot

Any Main DPS + Stunner + Nicole

Nicole's DEF shred is element-agnostic — she fits into ice teams (with Ellen or Miyabi), fire teams (with Soldier 11), electric teams (with Grace), and physical teams (with Billy or Piper). In every case, the damage formula works out to approximately 18-22% team DPS increase during her shred window. The only teams where Nicole is suboptimal are those that specifically need faction resonance or already have a dedicated support with higher synergy (like Soukaku in ice teams).

🎮 Playstyle & Rotation

Nicole's gameplay is centered around quick-swap support with minimal field time:

  1. Open with E: Start every engagement by tapping Nicole's Special Attack to deploy the Ether gravity field. This applies the DEF shred debuff immediately and groups enemies together.
  2. Swap to Stunner: While the gravity field is active (8 seconds), swap to your stun character to build daze. The DEF shred persists even while Nicole is off-field.
  3. Refresh on Cooldown: Swap back to Nicole when her E is off cooldown (12 seconds). There will be a 4-second gap where DEF shred is down — time this during boss phase transitions or when you're dodging attacks.
  4. Ultimate Usage: Use Nicole's Ultimate either for emergency crowd control (pulling scattered enemies) or to refresh the DEF shred buff during a stun window for maximum burst synergy.
  5. Key Optimization: Hold Nicole's E for 0.5 seconds before releasing — the charged version creates a larger gravity field with 15% more radius. This makes a significant difference in grouping efficiency against spread-out enemy waves.

💡 Pro Tip: Cancel Nicole's casting animation by swapping characters immediately after the gravity field appears. The field persists for its full duration even if you swap off Nicole instantly. This is the core tech that makes her a true quick-swap support.

❓ FAQ

Q1: What is Nicole's role and is she worth building?

Nicole is an A-rank Ether Support who provides group DEF shred (approximately 43% at max level), gravity pull crowd control, and Ether damage amplification. She is absolutely worth building — she's given for free, her DEF shred works with every damage type, and she fits into almost any team. She is one of the few A-rank characters that remains meta-relevant even in late-game content.

Q2: What's the best team for Nicole without Zhu Yuan?

Without Zhu Yuan, Nicole works best in the Cunning Hares team (Billy + Anby + Nicole) or as a universal support in any team lacking a dedicated support. She pairs particularly well with Soldier 11 (fire team) or Ellen Joe (ice team) because her DEF shred amplifies elemental damage types that already have good reaction support. The damage gain is roughly 18-22% regardless of the main DPS element.

Q3: How much Energy Regen does Nicole need?

Nicole needs approximately 2.2-2.5 total Energy Regen (including Swing Jazz 4-piece bonus) to maintain close to 100% uptime on her DEF shred field. With Swing Jazz (20% Energy Regen) + Slot VI Energy Regen main stat + Slice of Time passive, you'll comfortably hit this threshold. Without sufficient Energy Regen, there will be 6-8 second gaps between fields where your team deals significantly less damage.

Q4: Nicole vs Soukaku — which support should I build?

Build both if possible. Nicole provides universal DEF shred that benefits any team, while Soukaku specializes in ATK buffs with ice team synergy. Nicole is better in mixed-element teams or as a general support slot; Soukaku is superior in dedicated ice teams (with Ellen or Miyabi). If you had to choose one for early game, Nicole offers more flexibility across different team compositions.
