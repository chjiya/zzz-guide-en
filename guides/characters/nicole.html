Nicole Demara Build Guide 💼

📌 v1.0 • Updated June 4, 2026 • Ether • Support • A-Rank

Overview

Nicole Demara is an A-Rank Ether Support agent and the de facto leader of the Cunning Hares. Despite her free starter status, Nicole is one of the most valuable Support agents in Zenless Zone Zero — not just for F2P players, but for optimized endgame teams as well. Her kit provides two forms of universal damage amplification: DEF shred (enemies take increased damage from all sources) and enemy grouping via her black hole EX Special. These debuffs are element-agnostic, meaning Nicole amplifies any DPS character, not just Ether teammates.

Nicole's signature ability is her EX Special — "Energy Field" — which fires a gravitational orb that pulls enemies toward its center and continuously deals Ether damage. The grouping effect is invaluable for AoE DPS characters like Billy or Miyabi, and the persistent Ether damage helps build Ether Anomaly for Corrupted procs. Her Additional Ability is where she becomes truly exceptional: when paired with another Ether agent (like Zhu Yuan) or a Cunning Hares member, Nicole applies a 40% Ether RES shred to enemies hit by her EX Special — the single largest RES debuff in the game.

What makes Nicole an S-tier Support despite being an A-Rank is her consistency and low investment requirements. Her DEF shred and grouping work at level 1 with no gear. Her Core Passive unlocks provide incremental improvements rather than mandatory breakpoints. This means Nicole functions at 80% of her maximum potential with minimal resources, freeing you to invest heavily in your DPS and Stun agents instead. For any account — new or veteran — Nicole is always worth building.

Main Stat Recommendations

  • Disc 4: Anomaly Proficiency — helps Nicole apply Ether Anomaly (Corruption) faster, which provides additional damage and stagger.
  • Disc 5: Ether DMG Bonus or ATK% — Ether DMG amplifies her EX Special ticks; ATK% provides universal scaling. Either is fine since Nicole's personal damage is secondary.
  • Disc 6: Energy Regen — mandatory. Nicole's value is tied to her EX Special frequency. More Energy = more DEF shred uptime = more team damage.

Substat Priority

  1. Energy Regen — every point means faster EX Special rotation
  2. Anomaly Proficiency — faster Ether Anomaly application for Corruption procs
  3. ATK% — minor boost to her negligible damage
  4. PEN — helps her EX Special ticks deal slightly more damage

Best Team Compositions

Premium Ether Team (Zhu Yuan)

  • Zhu Yuan (DPS) — Ether Attack agent. Nicole's 40% Ether RES shred is purpose-built for her
  • Qingyi or Anby (Stun) — Fast Daze for frequent Stun windows
  • Nicole (Support) — Ether RES shred, DEF shred, grouping, and Cunning Hares faction synergy

This is Nicole's strongest team by far. The 40% Ether RES shred from Nicole's Additional Ability effectively multiplies Zhu Yuan's damage by 1.4x. Combined with Nicole's DEF shred and grouping, this team achieves the highest single-target Ether damage in the game. Rotation: Nicole EX Special (group + debuff) → Stun agent builds Daze → Stun triggers → Zhu Yuan burst.

F2P Universal Team

  • Corin or Billy (DPS) — Physical DPS, benefits from Nicole's universal DEF shred
  • Anby (Stun) — Free Stun agent, Cunning Hares faction synergy with Nicole
  • Nicole (Support) — DEF shred and black hole grouping for AoE DPS

Flex Support Slot

  • Any DPS (Miyabi, Ellen, Soldier 11, Nekomata)
  • Any Stun Agent
  • Nicole (Support) — Universal DEF shred works for all elements. Grouping helps any AoE DPS

Nicole's universal debuffs mean she has value even without Ether synergy. The 40% Ether RES shred only activates with an Ether teammate, but her DEF shred (25-40% depending on skill level) applies to all damage. The black hole grouping alone justifies her slot in multi-wave content like Hollow Zero.

Combat Rotation & Tips

  1. Opener: Start with Nicole. Fire EX Special (Energy Field) into the enemy pack. The gravitational pull will group them together. This is your debuff window — all enemies in the field take increased damage.
  2. Quick Swap: Immediately swap to your Stun agent. Nicole does not stay on-field — her job is done once the Energy Field is placed.
  3. Daze Phase: Your Stun agent builds Daze while enemies remain grouped and debuffed by Nicole's field. The field persists for ~8 seconds.
  4. Pre-Stun Refresh: When Daze is near full, swap back to Nicole and fire another EX Special. This refreshes the DEF shred for the upcoming Stun window.
  5. Stun Window: Chain Attack into your DPS (not Nicole). The DPS capitalizes on the refreshed DEF shred and the 150% Stun multiplier.
  6. Repeat: After the Stun window, start the cycle again. Nicole's Energy should be back by the time you need to reapply debuffs.

Key Tips: Nicole's Energy Field has a maximum range — position yourself so the orb lands in the middle of the enemy pack for maximum grouping. The field deals damage in ticks and each tick applies Ether Buildup, so it also contributes to Corruption anomaly. In teams without a dedicated healer, Nicole's grouping keeps enemies clustered and predictable, reducing the need to dodge multiple attack patterns simultaneously.

Mindscape Cinema (Duplicates) Value

  • M1: EX Special Energy cost reduced by 15%. ~17% more frequent debuff application. Excellent QoL and uptime improvement.
  • M2: Core Passive DEF shred increased by 8%. Direct amplification of Nicole's primary contribution. ~8% team damage increase against debuffed enemies.
  • M4: EX Special duration extended by 3 seconds (from 8s to 11s). Longer debuff windows means easier timing for the DPS burst phase.
  • M6: Enemies in Energy Field take 20% increased CRIT DMG. This is a unique debuff that stacks multiplicatively with DEF shred. ~15-20% team DPS increase during Stun windows. Transformative.

Nicole's Cinema levels are universally strong — every upgrade improves her support capabilities. M1 and M2 are easily achievable on any account. M6 is one of the best A-Rank M6 effects in the game, providing a rare CRIT DMG debuff that dramatically amplifies burst damage during Stun windows.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best W-Engine for Nicole?

Nicole's best W-Engine is The Vault, providing Energy Regen and a team-wide ATK buff after using an EX Special — perfectly aligned with Nicole's rotation pattern of using EX Special before every DPS burst window. The craftable Slice of Time is the best F2P option, also providing Energy Regen and a stacking team ATK buff from Chain Attacks and EX Specials.

Q: Which Drive Discs should Nicole use?

The optimal setup is 4-piece Swing Jazz and 2-piece Chaos Jazz. Swing Jazz provides Energy Regen for faster rotations and a team-wide 15% DMG buff after Chain Attacks — which Nicole triggers at the start of every Stun window. Chaos Jazz provides Anomaly Proficiency that helps Nicole apply Ether Anomaly more quickly for additional damage and stagger effects.

Q: What are Nicole's best teammates?

Nicole is the premier partner for Zhu Yuan, as her 40% Ether RES shred (Additional Ability) dramatically amplifies Zhu Yuan's Ether damage. She also excels in F2P teams with Corin/Billy and Anby — her DEF shred and black hole grouping benefit every Physical DPS. Her universal debuffs make her a valuable flex Support in virtually any team composition regardless of element.

Q: How does Nicole's DEF shred work?

Nicole's DEF shred reduces the target's DEF stat by a percentage (25-40% depending on skill level), which directly increases all damage dealt to that enemy. Unlike ATK buffs that add to an existing pool, DEF shred is a separate multiplier that scales multiplicatively with other damage buffs. This is why Nicole amplifies damage even in teams without Ether synergy — DEF reduction benefits everyone equally.

Q: Is Nicole worth building if I don't have Zhu Yuan?

Absolutely. Nicole's DEF shred (from EX Special) is universal and benefits every DPS regardless of element. The Ether RES shred from her Additional Ability is Zhu Yuan-specific, but the DEF shred alone is 25-40% increased damage for the entire team. Her black hole grouping is also universally useful for AoE DPS agents and makes content with multiple enemies significantly easier to manage.

Q: How much Energy Regen does Nicole need?

Aim for at least 50% Energy Regen from combined sources (W-Engine, Disc 6 main stat, substats). This ensures Nicole can use her EX Special at the start of every rotation and still have enough Energy to refresh the debuff before the Stun window. With 50%+ Energy Regen, Nicole's EX Special will be available roughly every 12-14 seconds — perfectly aligned with typical Stun cycle timing.
