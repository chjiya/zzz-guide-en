📋 Character Overview

Qingyi is an S-rank Electric Stun character from the Criminal Investigation Special Team (CIST). She is widely recognized as the fastest stunner in Zenless Zone Zero, featuring a unique Charged State mechanic that dramatically accelerates her daze application.

Qingyi's unique mechanic is Thunderbolt Stance — when she holds her attack button, Qingyi enters a charged state where her basic attacks are replaced with enhanced lightning-fast strikes. Each charged hit applies significantly more daze than normal attacks. The charged state has three levels, and at maximum charge, Qingyi's final strike is an AOE electric blast that applies massive daze to all enemies in range. What makes her truly exceptional is that her charged state also has increased interrupt resistance, allowing her to power through enemy attacks and keep building stun.

Strengths: Fastest daze application in the game (30-40% faster than Lycaon); highest stun multiplier at 150% (team deals 50% more damage during stun windows); excellent energy economy — her charged attacks generate energy even without hitting enemies; can build daze while dodging (charged state persists through dodge if timed correctly). Weaknesses: Vulnerable during charge animation startup (first 0.5 seconds before interrupt resistance kicks in); personal damage is moderate — she's a stunner first, damage second; relies on her team to convert stun opportunities into damage; less effective against highly mobile enemies who dodge her charged strikes.

🤝 Team Compositions

Qingyi is the premier stunner for Attack-focused hypercarry teams. She creates long, high-multiplier stun windows for your main DPS to deal damage:

🥇 Classic Meta (Best)

Qingyi + Zhu Yuan + Nicole

This team dominated ZZZ's endgame meta for patches 1.0-1.4. Qingyi stuns enemies in 8-10 seconds (vs 14-16 seconds with other stunners). Zhu Yuan then unleashes her full burst rotation during the 150% stun multiplier window, dealing 2-3x her normal damage. Nicole's DEF shred amplifies both Qingyi's electric damage and Zhu Yuan's ether damage. This team consistently clears Shiyu Defense floors 30-60 seconds faster than alternative compositions.

🥈 Ice Attack (Strong Alternative)

Qingyi + Ellen Joe + Soukaku

Ellen Joe benefits tremendously from long stun windows to unload her Afterimage charges. Qingyi's faster stun rotations mean Ellen gets more burst windows per fight. Soukaku provides Ice DMG buff and ATK buff. While Lycaon would provide Ice RES shred (making mono-Ice stronger), Qingyi's faster stun speed actually results in higher total DPS in practice despite the lack of ice synergy.

🥉 Flexible Quick-Swap

Qingyi + Any Attacker + Any Support

Qingyi works with any Attack character. Her universal stun capabilities mean she can slot into any team that needs faster stuns. Pair her with Soldier 11 + Lucy for fire teams, or with Corin + Rina for physical teams. Her flexibility makes her the safest S-rank stunner investment in the game.

🎮 Playstyle & Rotation

Qingyi's gameplay is all about efficient charge management:

  1. Open with Basic Attack: Start holding the basic attack button immediately. Qingyi enters charged state after ~0.5 seconds — power through this startup window (dodge if necessary).
  2. Build Charges: Release at charge level 1 for a quick strike (~120% daze), level 2 for a medium strike (~200% daze), or level 3 for maximum daze (~350% daze with AOE). The optimal rotation for pure speed is: charge to level 2 → release → immediately start charging again.
  3. Use EX Special for Burst: When you have Energy, use EX Special Attack. This instantly enters charge level 2 and deals burst daze. Follow up with a quick Level 3 charge for maximum daze output.
  4. Dodge Without Losing Charge: Qingyi can dodge while in charged state. A perfectly timed dodge will maintain your current charge level — this is the key skill that separates average Qingyi players from great ones.
  5. Stun Window: Once the enemy is stunned, swap immediately to your main DPS. Qingyi stun windows last ~5 seconds baseline — use the entire window for damage.

💡 Advanced Tech: Qingyi's dodge counter (perfect dodge → attack) instantly enters charge level 1. Use this aggressively against predictable boss patterns. Against the Dead End Butcher's charge attack, a perfect dodge into charged strike lets you counter-stun the boss before it recovers from its attack animation.

❓ FAQ

Q1: Is Qingyi worth pulling if I already have Lycaon?

Yes. While Lycaon is an excellent stunner, Qingyi is approximately 30-40% faster at building daze. The difference is noticeable in endgame content like Shiyu Defense, where faster stun rotations can save 30-60 seconds per floor. Additionally, Qingyi's 150% stun multiplier is higher than Lycaon's 125%, giving your team 25% more damage during the stun window. If you want to maximize your clear speeds, Qingyi is a significant upgrade over any other stunner.

Q2: What's the best Drive Disc main stat for Qingyi?

Impact% on Slot IV, Electric DMG% on Slot V, Energy Regen on Slot VI is the standard and best build. Impact% is non-negotiable on Slot IV — it directly affects her core function. Energy Regen on Slot VI dramatically improves EX Special uptime. Some players run ATK% on Slot VI for more personal damage, but this is a significant DPS loss for the team overall because slower stuns mean fewer burst windows.

Q3: Can Qingyi work as a sub-DPS instead of pure stunner?

Qingyi has moderate personal damage but is not designed as a sub-DPS. Her value is exclusively in her stun speed and stun multiplier. Building her for damage (CRIT stats instead of Impact) wastes her potential and results in slower team clears. If you want an Electric sub-DPS, Grace is a better choice. Qingyi should always be built for maximum stun speed.
