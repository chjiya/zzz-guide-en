Soldier 11 Build Guide 🔥

📌 v1.0 • Updated June 4, 2026 • Fire • Attack • S-Rank

Overview

Soldier 11 is an S-Rank Fire Attack agent and a member of the Obol Squad faction in New Eridu. As one of the most accessible top-tier DPS units (available from the standard banner), Soldier 11 has become a staple for many players' accounts. She wields a flamethrower-style weapon and specializes in sustained Fire damage over time through her unique Heat Gauge mechanic. Unlike burst-oriented DPS units, Soldier 11 thrives in extended combat where she can build and maintain her Heat Gauge, steadily ramping her damage output as fights progress.

The Heat Gauge is the centerpiece of Soldier 11's kit. When her Heat Gauge is active (triggered by using her EX Special), her Basic Attacks gain increased attack speed, additional Fire damage on every hit, and the ability to apply the Burn anomaly with dramatically increased efficiency. Managing this gauge — keeping it filled through continuous aggression while avoiding overheating (which triggers a brief cooldown period) — is the key skill that separates good Soldier 11 players from great ones.

Soldier 11's Additional Ability grants her increased Fire DMG based on her current Heat Gauge level, creating a rewarding feedback loop: the more aggressively you play, the more damage you deal. She also provides team utility through her Core Passive, which reduces enemy Fire RES by 15% when they are affected by Burn. This makes Soldier 11 an excellent driver for Fire-element teams and a strong partner for any Fire DPS or Anomaly agent. For players who pulled her from the standard banner or lost a 50/50 to her, investing in Soldier 11 is a decision that pays dividends across all game modes.

Main Stat Recommendations

  • Disc 4: CRIT DMG — Soldier 11 gets massive CRIT Rate from her Inferno Metal set (up to 36% total with Woodpecker). Stack CRIT DMG to capitalize on this. Aim for a 70:140 crit ratio or better.
  • Disc 5: Fire DMG Bonus — mandatory for any elemental DPS. This is Soldier 11's biggest multiplier.
  • Disc 6: ATK% — straightforward DPS stat. Energy Regen is not needed since her Heat Gauge management is more about timing than Energy availability.

Best Team Compositions

Premium Mono-Fire

  • Soldier 11 (DPS) — Primary on-field carry managing Heat Gauge
  • Koleda (Stun) — Fire Stun agent with the fastest Daze application among Fire units. Her Additional Ability grants bonus Daze against Burning enemies, which Soldier 11 provides naturally
  • Lucy (Support) — Fire Support who grants team-wide ATK buffs through her Guard Boar companion. Her Fire DMG Bonus passive benefits the entire team

This is Soldier 11's definitive team composition. The triple-Fire synergy means all Additional Abilities are active. Koleda builds Daze rapidly (and even faster against Soldier 11's Burning targets), Lucy provides consistent ATK buffs with near-100% uptime through her Guard Boar's attacks, and Soldier 11's Fire RES shred amplifies the entire team's damage during Stun windows. The rotation flows naturally: Lucy summons Guard Boar → Koleda builds Daze → Stun triggers → Chain Attack through all three agents → Soldier 11 activates Heat Gauge and unleashes enhanced Basics.

Budget-Friendly Team

  • Soldier 11 (DPS)
  • Anby (Stun) — Reliable free Stun agent; lacks Fire synergy but provides consistent Daze
  • Lucy or Nicole (Support) — Lucy for ATK and Fire synergy; Nicole for DEF shred and grouping

Anomaly Hybrid Team

  • Soldier 11 (DPS/Burn Driver)
  • Grace (Anomaly) — Applies Shock for Disorder reactions; Soldier 11's Burn provides the first Anomaly trigger
  • Rina (Support) — PEN Ratio buffs and Shock duration extension for more Disorder procs

This team leverages Soldier 11's rapid Burn application to set up Disorder reactions with Grace's Shock. Disorder deals massive damage when two different Anomalies are applied to the same enemy, and this duo can trigger it repeatedly. Effective in Hollow Zero and against high-HP bosses.

Combat Rotation & Tips

  1. Opener: Start with Soldier 11. Use her EX Special immediately to activate Heat Gauge Level 1. Her Basic Attack speed and damage are now enhanced.
  2. Gauge Management: While the Heat Gauge is active, sustain it by landing Basic Attacks (each hit adds a small amount of gauge) and using EX Special on cooldown (adds a large chunk of gauge). The gauge has three levels: Level 1 (25% bonus Fire DMG), Level 2 (50% bonus), and Level 3 (75% bonus, but gauge drains faster). Aim to maintain Level 2 for the best balance of damage and sustainability.
  3. Overheat Prevention: If the gauge reaches maximum, Soldier 11 enters Overheat state for 5 seconds, during which her attack speed is reduced. To prevent this, briefly swap to your Stun or Support agent when the gauge is nearly full — this pauses gauge accumulation and lets it cool down slightly.
  4. Support Buffs: When Lucy's Energy is full, swap to her and use her EX Special to deploy Guard Boar, which provides a team-wide ATK buff for 15 seconds. Time this just before a Stun window for maximum value.
  5. Stun Window Burst: Use Koleda (or Anby) to build Daze. When the enemy is stunned: refresh Lucy's ATK buff → Chain Attack into Soldier 11 → use her Ultimate if available (instantly maxes Heat Gauge and applies Burn) → EX Special → enhanced Basic Attacks until the Stun window ends. During Stun, let the gauge reach Level 3 for maximum burst damage.
  6. Post-Stun Reset: After the Stun window, the Heat Gauge may be high. Swap to Koleda to start building Daze for the next cycle while Soldier 11's gauge cools down.

Advanced Techniques: Soldier 11's 2nd Basic Attack in her enhanced string has a brief windup — animation-cancel it by tapping Dodge during the windup, then immediately resume attacking for faster combos. Against stationary bosses, maintain close range to ensure all hits of her cone-shaped Basic Attacks connect. Her Burn application is not dependent on Anomaly Proficiency (it's baked into her Heat Gauge), so don't waste substats on Anomaly stats — focus purely on CRIT and ATK.

Mindscape Cinema Value

  • M1: Heat Gauge drain rate reduced by 30%, making Level 3 maintenance significantly easier. Excellent quality-of-life upgrade that also increases sustained DPS. ~12% damage increase from more Level 3 uptime.
  • M2: EX Special deals 40% increased damage when Heat Gauge is at Level 2 or higher. Strong burst damage boost for Stun windows. ~10% DPS gain.
  • M4: Burning enemies take 15% increased CRIT DMG from Soldier 11. Massive damage amplification since Burn has near-100% uptime. ~18% DPS increase.
  • M6: During Overheat, Soldier 11 instead gains 30% increased ATK and attack speed for 8 seconds, completely inverting the Overheat penalty into a reward. Transforms her risk-reward dynamic and dramatically increases her damage ceiling.

M1 is the most impactful early Cinema level for quality of life. M4 provides the largest raw damage increase. M6 fundamentally changes how you play Soldier 11 and is worth pursuing for dedicated mains.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Soldier 11's best W-Engine?

The Brimstone is Soldier 11's best-in-slot W-Engine, providing ATK% and a stacking ATK buff on hit that synergizes perfectly with her rapid attack speed during Heat Gauge. For F2P players, the craftable Starlight Engine and Battle Pass Cannon Rotor are strong alternatives.

Q: Which Drive Discs should Soldier 11 use?

The recommended setup is 4-piece Inferno Metal and 2-piece Woodpecker Electro. Inferno Metal provides Fire DMG Bonus and a massive 28% CRIT Rate against Burning enemies, which Soldier 11 applies consistently. Combined with Woodpecker Electro's 8% CRIT Rate, she reaches 36% conditional CRIT Rate from set bonuses alone.

Q: Who are Soldier 11's best teammates?

Soldier 11's strongest team is with Koleda (Fire Stun) and Lucy (Fire Support). This mono-Fire core has full elemental synergy and covers all essential roles. Budget alternatives include Anby for Stun and Nicole for DEF shred.

Q: How do I manage Soldier 11's Heat Gauge?

Activate the Heat Gauge with EX Special. Maintain it at Level 2 (50% bonus Fire DMG) for balanced damage and sustainability. Use EX Special on cooldown to add gauge, and swap to another agent when the gauge is nearly full to prevent Overheat. During Stun windows, let it reach Level 3 for maximum burst damage.

Q: Is Soldier 11 worth building if I already have other Fire DPS?

Yes. Soldier 11 is unique among Fire DPS for her sustained damage profile and team-wide Fire RES shred. Even if you have limited Fire DPS options, her Burn application and Fire RES debuff make her valuable as a driver for Fire-element teams. She also performs exceptionally well in Hollow Zero where longer fights favor her ramp-up playstyle.

Q: Should I use CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG on Disc 4?

Use CRIT DMG on Disc 4. Soldier 11 gets massive CRIT Rate from her Inferno Metal set (28% conditional) plus Woodpecker Electro (8%), totaling 36% from set bonuses alone. With a base 5% CRIT Rate and substats, you can easily reach 60%+ CRIT Rate without a CRIT Rate main stat. Stack CRIT DMG to maximize her damage output.
