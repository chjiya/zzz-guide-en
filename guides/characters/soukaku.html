Soukaku Build Guide ❄️

📌 v1.0 • Updated June 4, 2026 • Ice • Support • A-Rank

Overview

Soukaku is an A-Rank Ice Support agent and a member of Hollow Special Operations Section 6. Don't let her small stature fool you — Soukaku is arguably the single strongest ATK buffer in Zenless Zone Zero, capable of providing over 1000 flat ATK to her teammates through her unique Vortex mechanic. Her role in the meta is cemented as the irreplaceable Support piece of the mono-Ice team archetype (alongside Miyabi/Ellen and Lycaon), which consistently ranks as the strongest team composition across all game modes.

Soukaku's kit revolves around a two-step buffing mechanic. First, she builds "Vortex" stacks by holding her Basic Attack or using her EX Special — during this time, she spins her massive naginata and absorbs energy. Then, she consumes all Vortex stacks with "Fly the Flag" (her EX Special's second activation), which plants her banner and grants the entire team a massive flat ATK buff proportional to the number of Vortex stacks consumed. At 3 stacks, this buff can exceed 1000 flat ATK — an enormous boost considering most endgame characters have 2500-3500 ATK total. This single buff can increase a DPS agent's damage by 30-40%.

Beyond her ATK buff, Soukaku provides additional Ice-specific support. Her Additional Ability grants 20% Ice DMG Bonus to all Ice-element teammates, making her the definitive Support for Ice DPS. At higher Cinema levels, she also gains minor healing capabilities, further cementing her value in extended content like Hollow Zero. Her playstyle requires some timing mastery — the Vortex charging animation leaves her vulnerable, and mistiming Fly the Flag can waste the buff if it expires before your DPS can capitalize. But once mastered, Soukaku is the engine that drives Ice teams to their record-breaking clear times.

Main Stat Recommendations

  • Disc 4: ATK% — Fly the Flag's ATK buff scales with Soukaku's ATK stat. Maximum ATK means maximum buff value for the team.
  • Disc 5: Ice DMG Bonus or ATK% — Ice DMG amplifies Soukaku's own damage (including Fly the Flag's damaging component). ATK% provides slightly higher buff value. Personal preference.
  • Disc 6: Energy Regen — mandatory. More Energy = more Fly the Flag uses = higher ATK buff uptime = more team damage.

Substat Priority

  1. ATK% — directly increases Fly the Flag's buff value for teammates
  2. Energy Regen — faster Fly the Flag rotations
  3. Flat ATK — small but contributes to Fly the Flag's ATK scaling
  4. CRIT Rate/DMG — lowest priority; Soukaku's personal damage is secondary to buffing

Best Team Compositions

Premium Mono-Ice (S-Tier)

  • Hoshimi Miyabi or Ellen Joe (DPS) — Ice DPS, benefits from Soukaku's 20% Ice DMG Bonus
  • Von Lycaon (Stun) — Ice Stun, provides 25% Ice RES shred on stunned enemies
  • Soukaku (Support) — 1000+ ATK buff, 20% Ice DMG Bonus, Freeze enabler

This is the strongest team in Zenless Zone Zero. The damage amplification chain is multiplicative: Soukaku's ATK buff → Soukaku's Ice DMG Bonus → Lycaon's Ice RES shred → DPS burst during Stun window. Against Ice-weak enemies, this team achieves the fastest clear times in Shiyu Defense Critical Node. Rotation: Soukaku builds 3 Vortex stacks → Lycaon builds Daze → enemy stunned → Soukaku Fly the Flag → DPS burst window.

Budget Ice Team

  • Any Ice DPS — Even a budget Ellen Joe benefits enormously from Soukaku's buffs
  • Anby (Stun) — Substitute for Lycaon. Less Daze speed, no Ice RES shred, but still functional
  • Soukaku (Support) — ATK buff and Ice DMG Bonus work regardless of Stun agent

Universal ATK Support

  • Any DPS (all elements)
  • Any Stun Agent
  • Soukaku (Support) — The ATK buff is universal and benefits non-Ice DPS. You lose the 20% Ice DMG Bonus, but 1000+ flat ATK is still a massive damage increase for any character.

While Soukaku is strongest in Ice teams, her ATK buff alone makes her a viable Support for any team. The flat ATK buff is particularly valuable for characters with low base ATK or those relying on ATK-scaling abilities.

Combat Rotation & Tips

  1. Vortex Stacking (Downtime): While your Stun agent builds Daze, periodically swap to Soukaku and hold Basic Attack to charge Vortex. You need 3 stacks for maximum Fly the Flag value. The charging spin deals AoE Ice damage and provides super armor.
  2. Pre-Stun Preparation: When the Daze meter is at 80-90%, ensure Soukaku has at least 2 Vortex stacks. If not, use her EX Special for an instant 2-stack charge.
  3. Stun Window Opener: As soon as Stun triggers, swap to Soukaku (she should have 3 Vortex stacks ready). Press EX Special to activate Fly the Flag — she plants her banner and the entire team receives the massive ATK buff.
  4. Burst Window: Immediately Quick Assist or Chain Attack into your DPS. The ATK buff lasts 20 seconds — you have plenty of time for the full burst rotation.
  5. Chain Attack Order: In mono-Ice, use Lycaon as the first Chain Attack (applies Ice RES shred), Soukaku second (for damage and Freeze buildup), then Miyabi/Ellen as the third Chain Attack for the maximum Chain Attack multiplier bonus.
  6. Buff Maintenance: Soukaku's ATK buff has a 20-second duration but she can reapply it earlier. Monitor the buff icon on your DPS and refresh when it's about to expire — don't wait for it to fall off.

Key Tips: The held Basic Attack for Vortex charging makes Soukaku stationary and vulnerable. Always position her safely before charging — behind the enemy or during boss recovery animations. Fly the Flag has a significant animation lock; use it when you know the enemy won't interrupt. Soukaku's Core Passive at higher levels also provides a small heal when Fly the Flag is used — this can be lifesaving in Hollow Zero at deeper Corruption levels.

Mindscape Cinema (Duplicates) Value

  • M1: Vortex charge speed increased by 30%. Reaching 3 stacks is 30% faster — directly improves rotation speed. Excellent QoL.
  • M2: Fly the Flag ATK buff increased by 20%. At max level, this pushes the buff from ~1000 ATK to ~1200 ATK. Massive team damage increase. Highly recommended.
  • M4: Fly the Flag heals all teammates for 15% of Soukaku's ATK. Adds sustain to the mono-Ice team, which otherwise has no healing. Valuable for Hollow Zero.
  • M6: Fly the Flag also grants 20% CRIT DMG to all teammates. This stacks multiplicatively with the ATK buff and Ice DMG Bonus. Transformative — M6 Soukaku is competitive with limited S-Rank Supports.

Soukaku has one of the best Cinema progressions among A-Rank agents. M1 and M2 are easily achievable and provide significant upgrades. M6 transforms her from an ATK buffer into a complete damage amplification package (ATK + Ice DMG + CRIT DMG), making her competitive with premium S-Rank Support options.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best W-Engine for Soukaku?

Soukaku's best W-Engine is The Vault, providing Energy Regen and a team-wide ATK buff after using EX Special — perfect synergy since Soukaku uses Fly the Flag (EX Special) before every burst window. The craftable Slice of Time is the best F2P alternative with Energy Regen and stacking team ATK buff from Chain Attacks and EX Specials for consistent support value.

Q: Which Drive Discs should Soukaku use?

The optimal setup is 4-piece Swing Jazz and 2-piece Polar Metal. Swing Jazz provides Energy Regen for faster Vortex stacking and a team-wide 15% DMG buff after Chain Attacks. Polar Metal's 2-piece grants 10% Ice DMG Bonus which amplifies Soukaku's Fly the Flag damage and Ice Anomaly Buildup for more consistent Freeze application in Ice teams.

Q: What are Soukaku's best teammates?

Soukaku is the premier Support for all Ice DPS agents — Miyabi, Ellen Joe, and Lycaon. Her Additional Ability grants 20% Ice DMG Bonus to Ice teammates, and her Vortex → Fly the Flag combo provides up to 1000+ flat ATK to the entire team. She is a core member of the mono-Ice team archetype that consistently tops Shiyu Defense rankings across all patches.

Q: How do I build Vortex stacks efficiently?

Build Vortex stacks by holding Soukaku's Basic Attack (spinning animation) or using her EX Special (instant 2 stacks). For maximum efficiency: charge to 2 stacks with Basic Attack between rotations, then use EX Special for the third stack just before Fly the Flag. This conserves Energy while ensuring maximum buff value. At M1, the 30% faster charge speed makes stack building significantly more forgiving.

Q: Does Soukaku's ATK buff stack with other ATK buffs?

Yes, Soukaku's Fly the Flag provides flat ATK (e.g., +1000 ATK), which stacks additively with other flat ATK buffs and is then multiplied by ATK% buffs. Flat ATK is applied before ATK% multipliers, making Soukaku's buff particularly powerful when combined with ATK% buffs from W-Engines or Drive Discs. The 20% Ice DMG Bonus from her Additional Ability stacks multiplicatively with all ATK buffs.

Q: Is Soukaku worth building without an Ice DPS?

Soukaku's value drops significantly without Ice teammates — you lose the 20% Ice DMG Bonus from her Additional Ability. However, the 1000+ flat ATK from Fly the Flag is still a massive universal damage increase for any DPS. If you lack Nicole or other premium Supports, Soukaku remains a viable ATK buffer even in non-Ice teams. Prioritize building her if you have or plan to pull any Ice DPS.
