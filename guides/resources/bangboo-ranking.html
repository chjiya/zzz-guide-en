🤖 Bangboo System Overview

Bangboo are the robotic companion units in Zenless Zone Zero. They follow your team into combat and provide active support skills (summonable via the Bangboo button, deals damage or applies debuffs), passive buffs (stat bonuses to your squad), and faction synergies (bonus effects when paired with characters from the same faction).

Unlike characters, Bangboo are obtained from the Bangboo Channel (a dedicated banner that uses Boopons — a separate currency earned from Hollow Zero, events, and achievements). They are significantly easier to collect than characters: you can expect to get 1-2 S-rank Bangboo per month as a F2P player, and all A-rank Bangboo are free from the Battle Pass and events.

Key Bangboo Mechanics:

  • Active Skill: Triggered by pressing the Bangboo button (left bumper on controller, Q on keyboard). Has a cooldown of 15-25 seconds depending on the Bangboo.
  • Passive Modifier: A permanent stat bonus to your team (e.g., +10% Anomaly Proficiency, +8% ATK).
  • Faction Synergy: When paired with 2+ characters from the matching faction, the Bangboo's active skill gains bonus effects (e.g., extra damage, reduced cooldown, additional debuffs).
  • Bangboo Level: Bangboo can be leveled up to 60 using Bangboo EXP chips. Higher levels increase the active skill's damage and sometimes the passive modifier's value.

Bangboo Rarity: S-rank Bangboo have stronger active skills and better passives than A-rank. However, A-rank Bangboo are often better than mismatched S-rank Bangboo because faction synergy bonuses are significant (roughly 25-40% more effective active skills).

🆓 F2P & Budget Recommendations

As a F2P player, you won't have access to all S-rank Bangboo immediately. Here's a realistic acquisition order:

1. Get Electroboo (A-rank): This is your first priority. Electroboo is an A-rank Bangboo that you can get from the Battle Pass (free track) or the Bangboo Channel. It provides the universally useful Energy Regen buff and works in literally every team. It's arguably the best value Bangboo in the game considering its rarity.

2. Get Safety (S-rank): If you don't have Caesar, Safety is your next priority. The 40% damage reduction and 2-second stun on cast are invaluable for survivability. Even if you have Caesar, Safety stacks with her shield for near-invulnerability.

3. Get Plugboo or Rocketboo (S-rank): These are element-specific. If you play anomaly teams (Yanagi, Burnice), prioritize whichever matches your main team. If you play both, get Plugboo first (Electric teams are more common in the current meta).

4. Get Avocaboo (S-rank): Priority for Hollow Zero enthusiasts. The healing makes high-difficulty Hollow Zero runs much more consistent.

5. Fill out your A-rank collection: Magnetboo (grouping), Butlerboo (debuff cleanse), and Sharkboo/Devilboo (element-specific) are all worth having as situational options.

F2P Bangboo Income: You can earn approximately 30-50 Boopons per week from Hollow Zero points, events, and Battle Pass. The Bangboo Channel guarantees an S-rank within 80 pulls (80 Boopons per 10-pull, so 640 Boopons total). With passive income of ~150 Boopons per month, you can expect 1 S-rank Bangboo every 4-5 months, plus multiple A-rank Bangboo along the way.

❓ FAQ

Q1: Should I spend Boopons on the Bangboo Channel or save them?

Spend them. Unlike character banners where you might want to save pity, the Bangboo Channel has no 50/50 mechanic — every S-rank Bangboo you pull is guaranteed to be one of the featured Bangboo on the current banner rotation. There's no reason to hoard Boopons. However, if none of the currently featured S-rank Bangboo fit your teams (e.g., Resonaboo when you don't have an Ice team), you can skip that rotation and wait for Plugboo or Safety to rotate in.

Q2: Does Bangboo level matter significantly?

Yes, but less than character levels. A level 1 Bangboo still provides its full passive buff and faction synergy — only the active skill damage scales with level. For team-support Bangboo (Safety, Electroboo, Magnetboo), the level is less important. For damage Bangboo (Plugboo, Rocketboo, Resonaboo), leveling to 60 adds approximately 50-60% more active skill damage. Prioritize leveling damage Bangboo first, support Bangboo second.

Q3: Can I use two Bangboo in the same team?

No. Each team can equip exactly one Bangboo. The Bangboo is selected when you form the team and cannot be changed mid-fight. In Shiyu Defense, both of your teams need their own Bangboo equipped. If you only have 2-3 good Bangboo, that's fine — one goes on each team, and the third is a spare. You never need more Bangboo than the number of teams you use simultaneously.

Q4: Is the Bangboo Battle Pass (Premium) worth it?

The free Battle Pass gives you one random A-rank Bangboo and some Boopons. The premium Battle Pass ($10) gives an additional A-rank Bangboo of your choice, more Boopons, and Bangboo EXP materials. For F2P players, the free track is sufficient — A-rank Bangboo are not so much stronger than free options that you need to pay. For monthly pass holders, the premium Battle Pass is a decent purchase if you're actively collecting Bangboo, but skip it if you already have the A-rank Bangboo you need.
