📋 Drive Disc System Overview

Drive Discs are ZZZ's equipment system — equivalent to artifacts/relics in other gacha games. Each agent equips 6 Drive Discs organized into 3 pairs (2pc × 3) or a 4pc set + 2pc set. Discs have a main stat (fixed by slot), level (1-15), and up to 4 substats. Each disc costs progressively more to upgrade, making efficient resource allocation critical. Drive Discs are farmed at the Music Store using Battery (stamina), starting from IK 15.

🔑 Key Stat Categories

  • Offensive: ATK%, Crit Rate, Crit DMG, DMG Bonus%, PEN Ratio
  • Anomaly: Anomaly Proficiency, Anomaly Mastery
  • Stun: Impact
  • Support: Energy Regen, HP%, DEF%
  • Universal: ATK, HP, DEF (flat stats — generally worse than % versions)

🎲 Substats: What to Keep & What to Trash

Substats are the main source of late-game power: only ~15% of an agent's final stat sheet comes from main stats at max level, with substats making up the rest. Each disc can have 3-4 substats initially, gaining +1 substat every 3 levels (Lv. 3, 6, 9, 12, 15).

🔥 Substats Rule of Thumb: For Attack DPS, a disc with 3 useful substats is a "keeper." 4 useful substats is "god roll." For Anomaly agents, Anomaly Proficiency substats are so valuable that a disc with 2 AP rolls + 1 ATK% roll is already very good.

💰 Farming Efficiency & Resource Management

When to Farm

  • IK 15-34: Do NOT farm Drive Discs. Focus on character ascension and skill materials. Use the free 5★ discs from story rewards.
  • IK 35-44: Start farming the Music Store (Rift 5). Only farm for correct main stats — don't chase substats yet.
  • IK 45+: Rift 6 unlocked. Begin substat optimization. Use the self-modeling resin (artifact transmuter) on Slot 4 for the hardest-to-find main stat.

Recycling System

Don't discard unwanted discs directly — recycle them at Scott's Outpost for valuable materials:

  • Gold discs → 10 Gold per piece → Exchange for self-modeling resins or tuning calibrators
  • Purple discs → 3 Gold per piece → Not worth farming but worth recycling if you have extras
  • Blue/green discs → Vendor for 10 Dennies each → Minimal value

Daily Farming Route

  • Use your first 60 Battery daily on the Music Store (Drive Discs)
  • Use 30-60 Battery on character ascension materials (VR Device)
  • Reserve remaining Battery for skill materials (VR Device)
  • Spend recharge Battery (from coffee, events, etc.) on whatever you need most
