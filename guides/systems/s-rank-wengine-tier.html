💎 Pull Value — Must-Pull vs Skip

Here is our pull priority recommendation for limited W-Engines, assuming you have the character:

✅ Must-Pull (Huge Upgrade)

  • Flamemaker Shaker (Burnice): The only W-Engine that literally doubles a character's core mechanic. Burnice without her signature is good; Burnice with it is one of the best units in the game. If you plan to use Burnice long-term, this is the #1 W-Engine pull priority.
  • Blazing Laurel (Caesar): The 35% team ATK buff on shield is transformative. Caesar is already the best defensive support in the game; her signature turns her into a top-tier offensive buffer as well.

👍 Worth Pulling (Significant Upgrade)

  • Deep Sea Visitor (Ellen Joe): 25-30% upgrade over F2P. High Crit Rate stat is excellent. Worth it if Ellen Joe is your main DPS.
  • The Vault (Zhu Yuan): Same upgrade range as Deep Sea Visitor. Critical for burst DPS optimization.
  • Mark of Descent (Harumasa): Harumasa is given for free, so investing in his W-Engine is more palatable. Strong upgrade.

🤷 Skip Unless You Love the Character

  • Frostblade (Miyabi): Rainforest Gourmet is only 20-25% behind. Save for another character.
  • Sharpened Stinger (Jane Doe): Good but not transformative. Jane works well with craftable options.
  • Ice-Jade Teapot (Qingyi): The Precious Fossilized Core does 80% of the job for free. Hard to justify 75+ pulls.
  • Weeping Cradle (Nicole): Bashful Demon is competitive. Only pull if you're a pure Nicole main.

Final Recommendation: For F2P and light spenders, the general rule is: character constellations (Cinemas) are more valuable than signature W-Engines in most cases. The exceptions are Burnice and Caesar, whose signatures are transformative enough to prioritize over C1. For all other characters, save your pulls for new characters or limited banner reruns.

❓ FAQ

Q1: How many pulls does a W-Engine cost on average?

The limited W-Engine banner has a hard pity of 80 pulls (40 more than character banner pity of 90, but note character banner 50/50 means ~150 expected pulls for a character). The 75/25 odds on the W-Engine banner mean you have a 75% chance to get the featured W-Engine when you hit pity, and pity is at 80. Expected pulls for a featured W-Engine: approximately 55-65 pulls. This is significantly cheaper than getting a limited character (expected ~150 pulls with 50/50). However, the W-Engine banner doesn't have the same 'guaranteed carry-over' system as the character banner.

Q2: Are standard banner S-rank W-Engines worth using?

Yes, several standard banner S-rank W-Engines are excellent. The Restrained (Stun class) and Bashful Demon (Support class, though technically A-rank) are among the best in their categories. The standard S-rank Attack W-Engines are generally worse than limited options but still better than A-rank alternatives. If you get a standard S-rank W-Engine from your new player pulls or standard banner pity, it's a good account boost — don't be disappointed it isn't a character.

Q3: Should I refine (duplicate) W-Engines or keep copies?

As a general rule for F2P players: do not refine limited W-Engines. Keep copies for other characters. The stat increase from R1 to R2 is usually 8-12%, which is less valuable than having two characters with that W-Engine. For example, having two Starlight Engines (one for Ellen, one for Zhu Yuan) is more valuable than one R2 Starlight Engine. Only refines after you have enough W-Engines to equip all your frequently used agents. For standard banner W-Engines, since they can spook your pulls at any time, you can refine duplicates once you have a decent pool.

Q4: Which W-Engine should I pick from the selector box?

The free S-rank W-Engine selector from the new player quest (Inter-Knot level 30) gives you a choice between 5 standard S-rank W-Engines. The best pick for most accounts is The Restrained 💡 (S-rank Stun W-Engine with Impact% secondary). It's the most universal and provides the biggest power boost for your stunner. Second pick is any Attack class W-Engine if you lack one for your main DPS. Support and Defense standard S-ranks are less impactful since the craftable A-rank alternatives are very close in performance.

Q5: Do event/exclusive W-Engines outperform craftable ones?

Yes, limited event W-Engines (from battle pass or special events) generally outperform craftable A-rank options. The Marcato Desire (Battle Pass Attack W-Engine) is roughly 85% as good as a limited S-rank Attack W-Engine, making it the best value non-limited option for Attack agents. For Anomaly agents, the Rainforest Gourmet (craftable) is already excellent, so event options don't provide as much value. Always check if a battle pass or event W-Engine is right for your specific character before purchasing.
