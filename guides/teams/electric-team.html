Understanding Electric Teams in ZZZ

Electric teams in Zenless Zone Zero offer the most diverse playstyle options of any element. Unlike Fire teams that revolve purely around Burn uptime or Ice teams that focus on Freeze crowd control, Electric teams can be built two fundamentally different ways: traditional DPS-Stun-Support teams centered around Anton's burst damage, or Anomaly Disorder teams where Grace applies Shock to enable devastating Disorder reactions with Physical or Fire partners. This flexibility makes Electric the most versatile element for team building.

The Electric roster features strong agent diversity. Anton is the primary Electric DPS with a unique Burst Mode mechanic that transforms his drill into an electrified powerhouse. Grace is the premier Electric Anomaly agent, capable of applying Shock faster than any other agent applies their respective anomaly. Qingyi is the limited S-Rank Electric Stun with unparalleled Daze speed. Rina serves as the premium Electric Support, providing PEN Ratio buffs and Shock duration extension. Anby rounds out the roster as the free starter Stun agent who benefits from Shock synergy via her Additional Ability.

Shock, Electric's Anomaly effect, deals periodic damage and interrupts enemy actions — effectively providing light crowd control while dealing damage. The Thunder Metal Drive Disc set provides bonus ATK against Shocked enemies, making Shock uptime the central concern for Electric DPS teams. In Disorder compositions, Shock's long duration (10 seconds base) makes it the ideal "setup" Anomaly — apply Shock with Grace, then trigger Disorder with another Anomaly effect for massive percentage-based burst damage that scales with enemy HP.

S-Tier Electric Teams

1. Premium Electric Burst: Anton + Qingyi + Rina

Why it's S-Tier: Triple Electric with perfect role synergy. Qingyi's unmatched Daze speed means more frequent Stun windows. Rina's PEN Ratio buff is especially valuable for Electric teams because Shock damage ignores a portion of enemy DEF — stacking PEN Ratio on top of this makes Anton's burst damage extremely consistent against all enemy types. The Thunder Metal disc set's ATK bonus is permanently active with triple Electric agents maintaining Shock uptime.

Weakness: Qingyi is a limited S-Rank, making this team expensive. Anton, while powerful, is an A-Rank DPS whose ceiling is lower than limited S-Rank carries. This team is strong but not as dominant as mono-Ice (Miyabi) or premium Ether (Zhu Yuan) compositions.

2. Electric Disorder Premium: Grace + Piper + Rina

Why it's S-Tier: This is the premier Disorder team in ZZZ. Grace applies Shock in 4-5 seconds with high Anomaly Proficiency investment. Rina extends Shock's duration by 3 seconds, giving Piper a generous 13-second window to apply Assault and trigger Disorder. The Disorder damage is percentage-based, making this team scale exceptionally well into high-HP content like Hollow Zero deeper Corruption levels. The rotation is smooth and forgiving compared to timing-sensitive Stun-window teams.

Weakness: Requires both Grace and Rina (standard banner S-Ranks), making the team RNG-dependent. Lacks a Stun agent, so no 150% Stun multiplier — damage comes purely from Anomaly and Disorder procs. Less effective in Shiyu Defense time trials that reward burst damage over sustained output.

Shock Uptime & Thunder Metal Optimization

The Thunder Metal Drive Disc set provides bonus ATK against Shocked enemies. Maximizing its uptime requires:

  • Multiple Electric Agents: Each Electric agent applies Shock Buildup with every attack. Two or three Electric agents dramatically increase uptime.
  • Grace's Anomaly Proficiency: Grace with 300+ Anomaly Proficiency can apply Shock in 4-5 seconds and maintain near-100% uptime alone.
  • Rina's Shock Extension: Rina's Additional Ability extends Shock duration by 3 seconds (from 10s to 13s), dramatically improving uptime consistency.
  • Chain Attack Buildup: Chain Attacks apply a large burst of Electric Buildup. During Stun windows, the triple Chain Attack sequence provides enough Buildup to refresh Shock mid-combo.

Rotations & Gameplay

Electric Burst Rotation (Anton + Anby + Grace):

  1. Grace uses Basic Attacks and EX Special to apply Shock (enables Anby's bonus Daze and Thunder Metal)
  2. Anby builds Daze with enhanced efficiency against Shocked enemies
  3. When Daze near full, Grace refreshes Shock if needed
  4. Stun triggers → Chain Attack: Anby → Grace → Anton
  5. Anton activates Burst Mode and uses enhanced drill attacks for the full Stun window
  6. Anton Ultimate at end of Stun window for maximum damage
  7. Repeat from step 1. Ensure Shock is active before Anby begins building Daze

Disorder Rotation (Grace + Piper + Rina):

  1. Grace on-field, EX Special immediately → Basic Attack string to apply Shock
  2. As soon as Shock is applied, swap to Piper
  3. Piper uses EX Special to rapidly apply Assault. Disorder triggers on Assault application
  4. After Disorder, swap back to Grace to reapply Shock
  5. Rina uses EX Special between swaps to refresh PEN Ratio buff and provide energy particles
  6. Repeat the Grace → Piper → Rina cycle continuously

Electric Team Investment Priority

  • Thunder Metal Discs: Farm this domain for Anton. The 4-piece ATK bonus against Shocked enemies is his primary damage source. Aim for CRIT and ATK substats since Anton already gets bonus ATK from the set.
  • Freedom Blues for Grace/Piper: Both Anomaly agents want 4-Freedom Blues (Anomaly Proficiency + Buildup rate). This domain shares with Fanged Metal, making it efficient for Physical + Electric Disorder team farming.
  • Grace's Anomaly Proficiency: Target 350+ for consistent sub-5-second Shock application. This is the make-or-break stat for Disorder team performance — slow Shock application means slow Disorder cycles and low overall DPS.
  • Anton's W-Engine: The Brimstone (ATK% + stacking damage buff) or Starlight Engine (craftable F2P). Anton's Burst Mode attacks are rapid multi-hits that quickly max The Brimstone's stacks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best Electric team in ZZZ?

The best Electric team is Anton (DPS) + Qingyi (Stun) + Rina (Support). Qingyi provides the fastest Daze application in the game, Rina extends Shock duration and provides PEN Ratio buffs for consistent damage, and Anton's Burst Mode deals devastating Electric damage during Stun windows. For F2P players, replace Qingyi with Anby for a functional budget alternative that still activates Electric synergy and provides reliable Stun windows.

Q: How does Shock synergy work in Electric teams?

Shock is Electric's Anomaly effect that deals periodic damage and interrupts enemy actions. Key synergies include the Thunder Metal disc set (bonus ATK against Shocked enemies), Anby's bonus Daze against Shocked targets, and Rina's Shock duration extension from 10 to 13 seconds. Maintaining consistent Shock uptime through multiple Electric agents is essential for maximizing these conditional damage bonuses that define Electric team performance.

Q: What is the best Electric Disorder team?

The best Electric Disorder team is Grace (Shock applicator) + Piper (Assault applicator) + Rina (Support). Grace applies Shock rapidly with high Anomaly Proficiency investment, Piper triggers Assault for massive Disorder burst damage, and Rina extends Shock duration for easier Disorder timing while providing PEN Ratio buffs. This team excels in Hollow Zero and against high-HP bosses thanks to Disorder's percentage-based damage scaling with enemy health pools.

Q: Is Anton worth building as an Electric DPS?

Yes, Anton is worth building if you lack limited S-Rank DPS options. His Burst Mode during Stun windows deals competitive damage, and his A-Rank status means Cinema levels come naturally. However, his damage ceiling is lower than limited DPS characters. He is best viewed as a strong transitional DPS — invest enough to clear content, but be ready to replace him when a limited Electric DPS releases.

Q: How important is Rina for Electric teams?

Rina is highly valuable but not mandatory for Electric teams. Her Shock duration extension (+3 seconds) dramatically simplifies Disorder timing and improves Thunder Metal uptime. Her PEN Ratio buff is universally strong, especially against high-DEF enemies. However, Lucy can substitute for ATK buffs in Disorder teams, and Nicole provides universally useful DEF shred. Rina elevates Electric teams from A-Tier to S-Tier but is not required for viability.

Q: What is the difference between Electric burst and Disorder teams?

Electric burst teams (Anton-focused) follow the traditional DPS-Stun-Support structure: build Daze, trigger Stun window, burst with DPS during the 150% damage multiplier. Disorder teams (Grace-focused) skip Stun agents entirely and deal damage by applying Shock + another Anomaly to trigger Disorder, which deals percentage-based burst damage. Burst teams excel in time-gated content; Disorder teams excel in long-form content with high-HP enemies like Hollow Zero Corruption runs.
