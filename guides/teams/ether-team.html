🔮 Ether Element Overview

Ether is a unique element in Zenless Zone Zero with a special property: it has no innate damage advantage or disadvantage against any enemy type. Unlike Fire (strong vs Ice enemies), Electric (strong vs Mechanical), or Physical (no advantage), Ether deals consistent, unresisted damage to ALL enemy types. This makes Ether teams incredibly versatile — you can bring them into any fight without worrying about element matching.

Ether Anomaly — Corruption: The Ether anomaly effect is Corruption. When the Ether Anomaly bar fills on an enemy, they take a burst of Ether damage and become Corrupted, which increases all damage they take from all sources by 10-15% for a duration depending on the agent's Anomaly Mastery. This makes Ether a fantastic element for team-wide damage amplification — even if your main DPS isn't Ether, a Nicole providing Corruption can boost the whole team's damage.

Current Ether Roster: As of v1.4, only two agents use the Ether element: Zhu Yuan (S-Rank, Attack, Main DPS) and Nicole Demara (A-Rank, Support, Sub-DPS/Buffer). This limited pool means Ether teams are built around maximizing the Zhu Yuan + Nicole core, with a third flex slot for a stunner or defensive support.

Key Strengths:

  • Universal Coverage: No bad matchups — Ether deals full damage to everything
  • DEF Shred Synergy: Nicole's EX Special reduces enemy DEF by 40% for 3.5 seconds, which is one of the strongest debuffs in the game
  • Burst Damage Profile: Zhu Yuan's kit is built around massive burst windows during stuns, making her one of the best Shiyu Defense clearing agents
  • Strong F2P Core: Nicole is an A-rank agent you get for free, and her C6 (M6) is one of the best support constellations in the entire game

🤝 Zhu Yuan + Nicole Core Synergy

The Zhu Yuan + Nicole duo is arguably the strongest 2-agent core in ZZZ. Here's why they work so well together:

1. Nicole's DEF Shred Amplifies Zhu Yuan's Burst: Nicole's EX Special creates a gravity field that reduces enemy DEF by 40% for 3.5 seconds. DEF reduction is a multiplicative damage amplifier — against a level 70 enemy, a 40% DEF shred translates to roughly 35-40% more damage for all team members during the window. Since Zhu Yuan's damage is front-loaded into her EX Special and Ultimate (both used during stun windows), this DEF shred window is perfectly aligned with her burst phase.

2. Energy Generation Loop: Nicole generates significant energy for the team through her EX Special hits and her C2 (reduces energy cost of her EX Special by 20% after a perfect assist). This energy helps Zhu Yuan fuel her high-cost EX Special spam.

3. Ether Corruption Amplification: Both agents deal Ether damage, so they build the Corruption anomaly bar quickly. Once the enemy is Corrupted (+10-15% all damage taken), both Zhu Yuan and Nicole benefit equally. Nicole's multi-hit EX Special is particularly good at building the Ether Anomaly bar.

4. Field Time Efficiency: Nicole requires very little field time — her EX Special is quick (1-2 seconds), and her chain attack hits hard without needing to stay on field. This gives Zhu Yuan maximum field time to build energy and deal damage.

👑 Premium Ether Team — Zhu Yuan + Nicole + Qingyi

Team: Zhu Yuan (Main DPS) + Nicole (Support/DEF Shred) + Qingyi (Stun)

This is the optimal Ether team and one of the strongest burst compositions in the game. Qingyi is the fastest stunner in ZZZ, building stun in 8-12 seconds against most enemies. This lets you cycle stun windows rapidly, aligning perfectly with Nicole's DEF shred and Zhu Yuan's energy economy.

Why Qingyi Over Other Stunners:

  • Fastest stun build: Qingyi's EX Special + enhanced basic attack combo builds 40-50% stun in one rotation
  • Energy generation: Qingyi generates energy for the team when she perfect-assists, supporting Zhu Yuan's EX Special spam
  • No overlap: Qingyi doesn't need to compete with Ether for debuff slots — she provides pure stun value
  • Comparable to Lycaon: Against non-Ice-weak enemies, Qingyi provides the same team DPS contribution as Lycaon would (since Lycaon's 25% Ice RES shred doesn't benefit Zhu Yuan's Ether damage)

Rotation: Qingyi builds stun (8-12s) → Apply Nicole DEF shred (during stun trigger) → Zhu Yuan EX Special spam + Ultimate → repeat. This team can 3-star any Shiyu Defense floor with proper execution at moderate investment.

🆓 F2P & Budget Ether Teams

Budget Option 1: Zhu Yuan + Nicole + Anby

Anby (A-rank Electric Stun, free) is a fantastic budget replacement for Qingyi. While her stun build is slower (14-18 seconds vs 8-12), her kit is otherwise solid for Ether teams:

  • Her EX Special has good stun multiplier and low energy cost
  • She generates energy on dodge counters, which are easy to land
  • At C6 (M6), her EX Special costs 20% less energy and deals 35% more stun damage

With Anby, your stun windows are less frequent but slightly longer (since Anby's stun duration bonus from C2 extends stun by 1 second). Adjust your rotation to use Nicole's DEF shred during the first 2 seconds of the stun window for maximum Zhu Yuan burst.

Budget Option 2: Nicole + Anby + Flex DPS (No Zhu Yuan)

If you don't have Zhu Yuan, Nicole + Anby + any attack DPS (Billy, Anton, etc.) still forms a competent team. Nicole's universal DEF shred benefits any damage type, and Anby provides the stun window for burst. This team won't clear endgame Shiyu Defense, but it's more than enough for story progression up to Inter-Knot level 50.

Free-to-Play Advice

Zhu Yuan was a limited banner S-rank, so if you missed her banner, she's currently unavailable. In that case, build Nicole to C6 and save your Polychromes for Zhu Yuan's first rerun. Nicole C6 is so strong that she's worth building even if you don't have Zhu Yuan — she slots into many non-Ether teams as a universal DEF shred support. Many top meta teams (like Caesar + Burnice teams) use Nicole as the third slot for the 40% DEF shred.

🔄 Optimal Rotation — Zhu Yuan Ether Team

Here is the step-by-step rotation for the premium Ether team (Zhu Yuan + Nicole + Qingyi):

  1. Stun Phase 1: Start with Qingyi. Use her EX Special + enhanced basic attack string to build the stun bar. This takes 6-10 seconds against normal enemies. Aim for 80% stun bar before your first damage window.
  2. Setup: When stun is at ~85%, swap to Nicole. Use her EX Special (creates gravity field + applies 40% DEF shred). The DEF shred lasts 3.5 seconds — time this so the shred is active when the enemy is stunned.
  3. Stun Trigger: Swap back to Qingyi for the final stun proc (EX Special hit that pushes stun to 100%). This triggers the chain attack prompt.
  4. Chain Attack 1: Select Nicole for the first chain attack (she applies more Ether damage + re-applies DEF shred if needed).
  5. Chain Attack 2: Select Zhu Yuan last for maximum field time. She enters the stunned phase with a full energy bar and both Nicole shred + corruption active.
  6. Burst Window: With Zhu Yuan on field: use Ultimate immediately → then spam EX Special 2-3 times (funds permitting) → weave in basic attack combos for energy generation. The stun window lasts 4-5 seconds.
  7. Recovery: After the stun ends, use Qingyi to rebuild stun and energy. Nicole swaps in briefly to maintain the team buff. Repeat from step 1.

Advanced Tips:

  • Save Zhu Yuan's Ultimate specifically for stun windows — using it outside of stun loses ~40% of its damage value
  • Always apply Nicole's DEF shred 0.5-1 second BEFORE the stun lands, so the debuff is active for the entire stun window
  • If Nicole has her C2, she generates extra energy on perfect assists — use her to parry enemy attacks for bonus energy generation
  • Qingyi's chain attack should be used last (before Zhu Yuan if possible) to maximize the chain attack multiplier on the stunned target

❓ FAQ

Q1: Is Zhu Yuan still meta in v1.4?

Yes, Zhu Yuan remains a solid S-tier DPS in v1.4. While newer characters like Miyabi and Caesar have higher ceiling potential, Zhu Yuan's burst damage profile is still excellent for Shiyu Defense speed clears. The main advantage of Zhu Yuan over newer DPS characters is her universal element — she never has a bad matchup. In the current Shiyu Defense meta, she clears at roughly the same speed as Ellen Joe and faster than Soldier 11. Her value is enhanced by Nicole C6 being widely available.

Q2: Should I pull for Zhu Yuan's signature W-Engine?

The Vault is Zhu Yuan's signature W-Engine and it provides approximately 25-30% more burst damage than the best F2P option (Starlight Engine). This is a significant upgrade, but not as dramatic as some other signature W-Engines (like Caesar's which is almost 40% better than alternatives). If you have limited pulls, prioritize getting Zhu Yuan herself (M0 or M1) before her W-Engine. The Starlight Engine is a craftable S-rank W-Engine that is roughly 80% as effective as The Vault.

Q3: Can Ether teams work without a stunner?

Technically yes, but not optimally. Zhu Yuan's kit is designed around stun windows — her entire damage profile is front-loaded into her EX Special and Ultimate, both of which benefit enormously from the 150% stun multiplier. Running her in a no-stun team (e.g., Zhu Yuan + Nicole + Caesar) sacrifices approximately 30-40% of her potential damage. However, Caesar's shield + ATK buff makes the team much more comfortable to play, so if you value survival over speed, this is a viable alternative. We recommend this setup for new players who haven't mastered dodge mechanics yet.

Q4: What's Nicole's minimum investment for Ether teams?

Nicole is usable at M0 but becomes significantly better at C2 (M2, EX Special costs 20% less energy after perfect assist) and C4 (M4, team regenerates 3 Energy over 12s after EX Special). Her C6 gives an additional 15% team Ether DMG bonus. The most important breakpoint is C2 for energy economy. In terms of levels, Nicole needs Level 50+ and Level 7+ EX Special for consistent DEF shred uptime. Prioritize her skill level over other substats.

Q5: What third slot is best for F2P Ether teams?

For F2P players without Qingyi or other S-rank stunners, the best third slot is Anby (free, A-rank Electric Stun). She provides reliable stuns and has good synergy with the burst-oriented Ether team. Other options include Lucy (A-rank Fire Support, provides ATK buff) if you want double support for more consistent damage, or Ben (A-rank Fire Defense) for survival-oriented clears. Note that double support sacrifices too much burst potential for Shiyu Defense, so Anby is almost always the superior choice.
