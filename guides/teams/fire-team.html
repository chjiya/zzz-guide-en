Fire Team Compositions Guide 🔥

📌 v1.0 • Updated June 4, 2026 • Fire • Endgame • Burn Synergy

Understanding Fire Teams in ZZZ

Fire teams in Zenless Zone Zero are defined by one central mechanic: Burn. Fire's Anomaly effect applies a damage-over-time status to enemies and, more importantly, enables a cascade of synergistic effects that dramatically amplify your team's damage output. The Inferno Metal Drive Disc set grants a massive 28% CRIT Rate against Burning enemies — the largest single CRIT Rate source in the game. Soldier 11's passive deals bonus damage to Burning targets. Koleda applies increased Daze against Burning enemies. Every Fire team revolves around maintaining Burn uptime to keep these powerful conditional effects active.

The Fire roster is compact but coherent. Soldier 11 is the primary Fire DPS and one of the original standard banner S-Ranks. Koleda is the premium Fire Stun agent whose entire kit is built around Burn synergy. Lucy serves as the Fire Support, providing team-wide ATK buffs and Fire DMG Bonus through her Additional Ability. This tight three-agent core forms the backbone of every competitive Fire team, with substitutions available for players missing Koleda or Soldier 11.

The core challenge of Fire teams is Burn uptime management. The Inferno Metal 4-piece bonus — 28% CRIT Rate — only works while the enemy is Burning. If Burn falls off, your DPS loses nearly a third of their CRIT Rate instantly. This means Fire teams need to apply Fire Anomaly Buildup consistently, not just occasionally. Soldier 11 alone can maintain ~60-70% Burn uptime. Adding a second Fire agent (Koleda or Lucy) pushes this to ~85%. For near-100% uptime, you need an Anomaly-focused build on your Support or a dedicated Fire Anomaly sub-DPS.

Burn Uptime & Inferno Metal Optimization

The Inferno Metal Drive Disc set is the foundation of Fire team damage. Its 4-piece bonus — 28% CRIT Rate against Burning enemies — is transformative. Here's how to maximize its uptime:

  • Triple Fire: Running three Fire agents (Soldier 11 + Koleda + Lucy) provides near-100% Burn uptime. All three apply Fire Anomaly Buildup with every attack.
  • Anomaly Proficiency on Lucy: Give Lucy as much Anomaly Proficiency as substats allow. Her Guard Boar's attacks and Basic Attacks contribute Fire Buildup. Every bit helps maintain Burn.
  • Chain Attack Sequencing: During Stun windows, use all three Chain Attacks. Each Chain Attack applies a significant burst of Fire Anomaly Buildup, refreshing or extending Burn.
  • Energy Management: Koleda's EX Special applies massive Fire Buildup. Use it not just for Daze but also to refresh Burn if you notice the debuff has fallen off.

Rotations & Gameplay

Mono-Fire Rotation (Soldier 11 + Koleda + Lucy):

  1. Lucy uses EX Special to summon Guard Boar and apply ATK buff to the team
  2. Koleda builds Daze with Basic Attacks. Her attacks also apply Fire Buildup to maintain Burn
  3. When Daze is near full, Lucy refreshes ATK buff if needed
  4. Stun triggers → Chain Attack sequence: Koleda → Lucy → Soldier 11 (maximizes Chain Attack multiplier for DPS)
  5. Soldier 11 activates Heat Gauge and uses enhanced Basic Attacks for the full Stun window
  6. Soldier 11 Ultimate at end of Stun window if available
  7. Repeat from step 1

Key Tips: Soldier 11's Heat Gauge management is the skill ceiling of Fire teams. Enter Heat state (enhanced attacks) only during Stun windows — using it outside wastes the state on unamplified damage. If Burn falls off mid-Stun window, use Soldier 11's EX Special to reapply it immediately — losing 28% CRIT Rate for even a few seconds is a significant DPS loss.

Fire Team Investment Priority

  • Inferno Metal Discs: Farm this domain first. Soldier 11's performance doubles once you have the 4-piece set active. Aim for CRIT Rate/DMG substats since you already get 28% CRIT Rate from the set bonus against Burning enemies.
  • Soldier 11's W-Engine: The Brimstone is her BiS (ATK% + stacking damage buff). Starlight Engine is the F2P alternative. Prioritize W-Engine levels — they provide the largest stat increases.
  • Koleda's Impact: Koleda wants maximum Impact for faster Daze. The Restrained (Impact main stat) and Shockstar Disco (Impact + Daze bonus) are essential.
  • Lucy's Cinema Levels: M2 extends Guard Boar's ATK buff duration (50% to 67% uptime). M4 adds healing. Both significantly improve team consistency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best Fire team in ZZZ?

The best Fire team is Soldier 11 (DPS) + Koleda (Stun) + Lucy (Support). This triple-Fire composition activates all three agents' Additional Abilities, providing Soldier 11's Burn-based damage amplification, Koleda's faster Daze against Burning enemies, and Lucy's team-wide Fire DMG Bonus and ATK buff. The Inferno Metal disc set's 28% CRIT Rate bonus is permanently active with three Fire agents applying Anomaly Buildup.

Q: How does Burn synergy work in Fire teams?

Burn is Fire's Anomaly effect that deals damage over time and is the central mechanic around which all Fire teams are built. Key synergies include the Inferno Metal disc set (28% CRIT Rate against Burning enemies), Soldier 11's bonus damage to Burning targets, and Koleda's increased Daze application against Burning enemies. Maintaining high Burn uptime through consistent Fire Anomaly Buildup is the primary strategic goal of every optimized Fire team.

Q: What is the best F2P Fire team?

The best F2P Fire team is Soldier 11 (standard banner DPS) + Anby (free Stun) + Lucy (A-Rank Support). While Soldier 11 requires luck from the standard banner, she's the most accessible Fire DPS. Lucy appears on all banners and provides essential ATK buffs plus Fire DMG Bonus from her Additional Ability. Anby fills the Stun role reliably despite lacking Fire synergy, making this the most practical Fire team for budget-conscious accounts.

Q: Is Soldier 11 worth building as a standard banner character?

Yes, Soldier 11 is one of the best standard banner DPS agents. Her Fire element has consistent Burn synergy and the Inferno Metal disc set (28% CRIT Rate) dramatically lowers her gear requirements. She excels in long-form content where her sustained damage profile has time to ramp up. While she won't match limited Fire DPS that release in future patches, she remains competitive through current endgame content with proper investment.

Q: How do I maintain Burn uptime in Fire teams?

Maintain Burn uptime by running multiple Fire agents (2-3) to maximize team-wide Fire Anomaly Buildup. Give your Support agent (Lucy) Anomaly Proficiency substats to increase their Fire Buildup contribution. Use Chain Attacks during Stun windows — each Chain Attack applies a significant burst of Fire Buildup that helps refresh or extend Burn. With triple Fire agents, Burn uptime should reach 90%+ in most encounters.

Q: Can I use Ben instead of Koleda in Fire teams?

Yes, Ben is a viable A-Rank alternative to Koleda. He provides a shield that significantly improves survivability, especially in Hollow Zero, and his Fire element maintains triple-Fire synergy. However, his Daze output is lower than Koleda's, resulting in slower Stun windows and longer clear times. Ben teams trade speed for safety — excellent for survival-focused content, suboptimal for time-gated Shiyu Defense Critical Node floors where speed matters most.
