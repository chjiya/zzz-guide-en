📋 How We Rank

This tier list ranks characters based on their performance in Shiyu Defense Critical Nodes (the primary endgame DPS check) and Hollow Zero at high difficulty levels. Rankings assume M0 (no mindscapes) with optimal F2P W-Engines unless specified. Characters are evaluated on: damage output (both burst and sustained), team synergy (how many teams they enable), utility (shields, buffs, debuffs, CC), and investment floor (how well they perform at minimal investment).

📊 Ranking by Role

Attack (Main DPS)

S: Zhu Yuan • A: Ellen Joe, Soldier 11 • B: Corin, Billy • C: Anton, Nekomiya

The Attack class is in a tough spot in the current meta. Anomaly and Disorder teams generally outperform pure Attack teams in sustained damage scenarios. Zhu Yuan remains S-tier because her burst damage during stun windows is so high that it overcomes the sustained DPS gap. Ellen Joe can still clear content but needs more investment to match Disorder team clear times.

Anomaly

S: Yanagi, Burnice, Jane Doe • A: Piper (M6) • B: Grace • C: None

Anomaly characters dominate the current meta. The combination of Anomaly Proficiency scaling, Disorder burst damage, and off-field application makes them the most efficient DPS class. Every S-tier team in the game features at least one Anomaly character, and most feature two.

Stun

A: Qingyi • B: Anby, Lycaon • C: None

The Stun class has lost some relevance in the Disorder era, but Qingyi remains excellent for burst comps. Anby is a budget queen. Lycaon (standard S-rank) is decent but offers nothing that Qingyi doesn't do better.

Defense

S: Caesar • A: Seth • B: None • C: Ben

Caesar single-handedly makes the Defense class relevant. Without her, it would be the weakest class in the game. Seth is a good budget alternative who specifically helps anomaly teams.

Support

A: Nicole, Lucy • B: Rina, Soukaku • C: None

Nicole and Lucy are the standout supports. Both are A-rank but perform at S-rank level within their respective team archetypes (Ether and Fire). Rina is a decent generalist but her PEN buff is less impactful than Nicole's DEF shred in most scenarios.

❓ FAQ

Q1: Is this tier list accurate for new players?

This tier list is optimized for endgame performance (Shiyu Defense S-rank clears, high-difficulty Hollow Zero). For new players, some rankings differ. For example, Anby is B-tier in endgame but S-tier for new players because she's free, easy to build, and works well with the starter team. Billy is C-tier in endgame but B-tier for early game. Focus on building characters that have both early-game accessibility and endgame potential — Piper, Lucy, and Nicole are your best early investments.

Q2: Why is Caesar S-tier but she deals no damage?

Caesar is S-tier because she provides the strongest defensive utility in the game while also giving a massive offensive buff. A 1,000 ATK team buff is equivalent to approximately 25-30% more damage for your main DPS. Combined with the game's best shield (allowing your DPS to play aggressively without dodging), Caesar effectively increases your team's DPS by more than a second DPS character would. She is the definition of a force multiplier.

Q3: Should I pull for Qingyi or save for a future S-rank stunner?

If you have Zhu Yuan, Qingyi is worth pulling — the Zhu Yuan + Qingyi + Nicole team is the best burst comp in the game and clears Shiyu Defense in 70-100 seconds. If you don't have Zhu Yuan or you prefer anomaly teams, skip Qingyi. Anby at high investment (M6 with a good W-Engine) performs at approximately 75-80% of Qingyi's daze output, which is sufficient for most content. The stun class may also see power creep in future patches.

Q4: How often is this tier list updated?

This tier list is updated for the current major patch (v1.4). It is re-evaluated whenever new characters are released or significant balance changes are made. Major shifts typically happen when new S-rank agents are introduced — for example, the addition of Yanagi and Burnice created the Disorder meta that now dominates the tier list. We recommend checking back after each major patch update.
